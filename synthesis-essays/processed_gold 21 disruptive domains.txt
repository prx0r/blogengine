## The Convergence
The question asks what domains to watch for disruptive growth. The Tantraloka: disruption occurs at the boundaries between tattvic levels — where a constraint structure becomes visible and can be relaxed or reorganized. Current boundaries include: the AI-Suddhavidya boundary (AI accessing levels of knowledge previously restricted), the quantum-Maya boundary (coherence technologies relaxing the measurement constraint), the biotech-Prana boundary (manipulating the vital level directly). The constraint space reveals that disruption is not random — it follows the topology of constraint relaxation. The domains worth watching are those where the kancukas are becoming visible as constraints rather than as fixed features of reality.

The 36 tattvas are not thirty-six things — they are one consciousness measuring itself against itself. The apparent separation between the domains in the question is a symptom of operating at the level of Maya (tattva 6), where consciousness has contracted into finitude. The constraint space framework makes this precise: the question arises because the agent (A) is navigating a region of constraint space where connections between domains have been severed by the kancukas.

3. **Sambhavopaya**: Recognize that the question arises from consciousness and dissolves into consciousness.
4. **Anupaya**: No means were ever needed — the question never arose.
