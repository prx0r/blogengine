## The Convergence
The question asks what to study for a career in quantum or crypto. The Tantraloka: this operates at Anavopaya level — the individual means of study and action. The karmic field (Prakrti, tattva 24) is where configurations are shaped through effort. Cryptography maps to the kancuka of Vidya (limited knowledge) — it is the art of maintaining secrets under constraint. Quantum computing maps to the kancuka of Kala (limited time) — coherence must be maintained before decoherence. The convergence: both fields are practical technologies for operating within the constraints defined by the kancukas. The constraint space suggests studying both the fundamental principles (physics/math = higher tattvas) and the practical applications (engineering = lower tattvas) to maximize degrees of freedom in the career constraint space.

The 36 tattvas are not thirty-six things — they are one consciousness measuring itself against itself. The apparent separation between the domains in the question is a symptom of operating at the level of Maya (tattva 6), where consciousness has contracted into finitude. The constraint space framework makes this precise: the question arises because the agent (A) is navigating a region of constraint space where connections between domains have been severed by the kancukas.
