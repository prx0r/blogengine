## The Convergence
Thai Puttapisek ceremonies charge amulets through spiritual intent of monks. The Tantraloka explains through sankalpa-sakti — the power of intention operating at Iccha level (will, tattva 3/Sadasiva). When consciousness is stable at Suddhavidya (tattva 5) or higher, the intentional configuration impresses itself onto the object through the consciousness continuum underlying both subject and object. Quantum information theory provides mechanism: the object quantum state becomes entangled with the monk intentional state. The constraint space describes this as transfer of constraint structure from A (agent at high recognition) to an object in L3.

The 36 tattvas are not thirty-six things — they are one consciousness measuring itself against itself. The apparent separation between the domains in the question is a symptom of operating at the level of Maya (tattva 6), where consciousness has contracted into finitude. The constraint space framework makes this precise: the question arises because the agent (A) is navigating a region of constraint space where connections between domains have been severed by the kancukas.
