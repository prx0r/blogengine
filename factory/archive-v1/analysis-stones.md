# Analysis: The Stones Are Watching You

**File:** `stones_are_watching_film_pack.zip` (48.7 MB)
**Type:** Film pack
**Duration:** 698s (11.6 min), 11 chapters
**Shots:** 106, 45 motifs

## What It Teaches

### 1. Motif Names Are Concrete Nouns
Every motif name evokes a specific IMAGE:
- `bishop_codex` (a manuscript page with writing)
- `watching_stones` (stones with eye-like formations)
- `inner_lattice` (crystal structure visible inside stone)
- `sulphur_fire` (yellow flame)
- `mercury_flow` (liquid metal cascading)

Compare to my motifs: `body_surface`, `mind_thought`, `witness_center` — abstract categories, not images.

### 2. Chapters Interleave
Chapter "I. The Patient Life of Stone" appears at shots 1, 5, 7, 51, 66, 71, 73, 82, 83, 91. It's scattered across the entire film. Chapter "IX. The Intermediate Substance" spans shots 2-106. The chapters are WOVEN TOGETHER, not presented linearly.

### 3. Raw Audio Duration Preserved
Every shot stores `raw_audio_duration` alongside the padded `duration`. This allows tracking of quantization error — the exact difference between natural speech and the rendered frame boundary.

### 4. 5 Continuity Rules
The visual_program defines 5 hard rules that constrain every visual decision:
- Stones reveal interior lattices
- Gold lines = formative/celestial causality
- Earth/water begin as fields, return in salts/vessels
- Stone-eye → bishop's eye → human form
- Alchemy = preparation, not forceful command

## Integration Into System

- Require concrete-noun motif names (if you can't picture it from the name, rename it)
- Interleave chapters — no one chapter occupies 3+ consecutive shots
- Store `raw_audio_duration` alongside `duration` in storyboard
- Add 3-5 continuity rules to every visual thesis
