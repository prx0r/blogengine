# After the Angel — Commentary

> Marcus Katz, *After the Angel: An Account of the Abramelin Operation* (2011)
> A first-person diary of the 6-month Abramelin working, completed in 2004.
> 7 years of integration before publication.

---

## The Book Is Sealed

Katz studied Abramelin for 20 years before performing it. He says the text
only opens when you are LIVING the Operation — the instructions that seemed
fragmentary or obscure become crystal clear once you are in the daily practice.
"This is the magick" — the book is designed to be unreadable until you need it.

## The Schedule (177 Days)

| Period | Duration | Practice |
|--------|----------|----------|
| First two moons | ~60 days | Daily prayer 15 min before sunrise + evening. 2 hrs study after dinner. Saturday: change bed linens, perfume chamber. |
| Second two moons | ~60 days | Increased prayer length. Wash hands/face before oration. Fast every Saturday. Withdraw from world. |
| Third two moons | ~60 days | Three prayers daily (dawn, noon, dusk). Constant incense. Anointing. Near-continuous prayer state. |
| Final 3 days | 72 hrs | Day 1: prepare oratory, absolute silence. Day 2: hours of prayer, fasting. Day 3: the Angel appears. |

## The Contact

Katz saw his Angel physically — "a vast swimming manta-ray shape of complex
wings and light" in the incense smoke. The presence was "timeless, wise,
powerful beyond any human measure." It was as curious about him as he was
about it — "I felt it was being introduced to me as much as I to it."

After the three-day contact, the next three days are spent invoking and
binding the four chief evil spirits (Lucifer, Leviathan, Belial, Satan)
and their sub-princes. The Operant also receives four familiar spirits
(one per element, rotating in 6-hour shifts).

## The Aftermath

The vacuum after completion was overwhelming. Katz describes a "divine
silence" in which the K&C grew like a new baby over nine months. It took
seven years to integrate. He went from IT Manager to therapist, teacher,
and writer. "When I commenced I was an IT Manager and occultist. A Shaman
in a Suit. Now I am myself."
