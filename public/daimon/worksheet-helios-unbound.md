□ Journal the session

## Month 3: Sacred Language

> Theme: Greek letters, voces magicae, vowel chanting.
> Candles: 28 white tea lights.

Each week works with a different set of Greek letters and their
numerical values. The core practice is chanting sequences of Greek
vowels (A-E-Ē-I-O-Y-Ō) in specific patterns to create vibrational
alignments with the Intelligible realm.

```
NIGHT SESSION (each day):
  □ Light white tea light
  □ Usual opening: LBRP, Sun Breath, Inner Temple
  □ Recite Invocation to the Sun (appropriate to month)
  □ Work with the current week's letter sequence:
    - Trace the letter in the air with your wand/dagger
    - Vibrate the letter's sound 7 times
    - Chant the vowel sequences provided in the text
    - Sense where the vibration resonates in your body
  □ Contemplate the philosophical meaning of the letter
  □ Prima Materia
  □ Journal: "Which letters resonated most strongly?"
```
