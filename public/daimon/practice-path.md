# The Daimon Path

For Lvahtzkel (LVJK / לוצכל). Mercurial daimon. Day birth.

---

## Why This Path

11 weeks. 3× daily. You compose your own invocation during the 11 weeks.
At week 12, you burn it. The Angel appears.

**Why 11 weeks?** Liber 8 says 11 weeks of thrice-daily prayer, then the
Angel appears when you burn your composed invocation. The Reddit
commentary (lib813) confirms: "Liber Samekh is a fairly simple ritual
that can be repeated easily and often with minimal tools." Not 6 months
of Abramelin purification. Not 11 months of Samekh escalation. 11 weeks
of focused, sincere work.

**Why 3× daily?** Liber 8 prescribes sunset, midnight, sunrise. The
Reddit commentator says "Invoke Often" means psyche yourself up to
invoke because you *want* to. Three times daily builds momentum without
burnout. Dawn, noon, dusk — three fixed points that structure your day.

**Why the Oath?** (Liber Samekh §A, lib813 commentary) — "The invocation
of the Bornless One sets up a supernal but intelligible godform for the
operator to identify with. At the initial stage, it's play-acting that
makes you seem qualified to ask for a meeting with HGA-level
intelligences."

**Why four archangels?** (lib813, adapted) — "An invocation of Air, for
which the operator should face East... Demonolators may wish to adapt the
language to invoke Oriens." Raphael, Michael, Gabriel, Auriel are the
standard Hermetic archangels. They work. The Reddit commentator says:
"Read this and the following sections carefully, the logic is important
to what Liber Samekh is trying to do."

**Why 5 PGM spells?** Each teaches a skill the 11-week operation
requires — voice (prayer), reception (dreams), intent in matter
(talisman), sustained focus (bowl), cosmic relationship (Helios).
The Reddit path says: "devotional and purificatory work alone" can
achieve K&C. These five are that work.

**Why compose your own invocation?** (Liber 8) — "During all this time
he shall have composed an invocation suitable, with such wisdom and
understanding as may be given him from the Crown." The Reddit
commentator says Section Aa "is all rather specific to Crowley's
self-conceptions as a magus. I would not use any of this verbatim."
You write the version that fits you.

**Why the Chaldean Oracles as verification?** (lib813) — "This lengthy
excerpt from the Chaldean Oracles is given for a reason... I believe this
is also offered as a description to check one's attainment against."

## The Daily Session (3×: dawn, noon, dusk)

Each session follows the same structure. Do not vary it.

### 1. The Oath (3 min)

Stand facing east. Candle lit. Recite from memory:

> Thee I invoke, the Bornless One.
> Thee, that didst create the Earth and the Heavens.
> Thee, that didst create the Night and the Day.
> Thee, that didst create the darkness and the Light.
> Thou art ASAR UN-NEFER: Whom no man hath seen at any time.
> Thou art IA-BESZ: the Truth in Matter.
> Thou art IA-APOPHRASZ: the Truth in Motion.
> Thou hast distinguished between the Just and the Unjust.
> Thou didst make the Female and the Male.
> Thou didst produce the Seeds and the Fruit.
> Thou didst form Men to love one another, and to hate one another.

### 2. The Four Pentagrams (12 min — 3 min each)

Face each quarter in turn. Draw the invoking pentagram in the air with
wand or finger. Vibrate the archangel's name while drawing. Visualize
the color. Say the charge. Trace a circle of light.

| Quarter | Element | Archangel | Color | Charge |
|---------|---------|-----------|-------|--------|
| East | Air | Raphael | Yellow | "Let understanding be my guide" |
| South | Fire | Michael | Red | "Let will be my strength" |
| West | Water | Gabriel | Blue | "Let love be my foundation" |
| North | Earth | Auriel | Green | "Let stability be my ground" |

*The Air Pentagram (East):* Start lower-left → top → lower-right →
upper-left → upper-right → back to start.

*The Fire Pentagram (South):* Start lower-right → top → lower-left →
upper-right → upper-left → back to start.

*The Water Pentagram (West):* Start top → lower-left → lower-right →
upper-left → upper-right → back to top.

*The Earth Pentagram (North):* Start top → lower-right → lower-left →
upper-right → upper-left → back to top.

After the fourth quarter, return to center.

### 3. The Name (5 min)

Vibrate LVAHTZKEL 7×. Each repetition extends your awareness further
along a shaft of light. By the 7th, your consciousness should feel
extended to its limit.

### 4. The Union (5 min)

From Liber Samekh Section G: hold the tension. Do not release. Let the
resistance build until the name vibrates on its own.

### 5. The Charge (2 min)

> Hear me, and make all Spirits subject unto Me:
> so that every Spirit of the Firmament and of the Ether,
> upon the Earth and under the Earth,
> on dry Land and in the Water,
> of Whirling Air and of rushing Fire,
> and every Spell and Scourge of God
> may be obedient unto Me.

### 6. Close

Extinguish candle. Write three lines in your journal:
- How did the name feel?
- Did any quarter feel different?
- Any images, words, or sensations?

### Dusk Session — Dream Seeding (additional step after Close)

After the standard session, add this for the dusk practice specifically:

7. **Dream Seeding (5 min).** Sit in the dark with the candle out. Write
   a single question to LVJK in your journal. Do not try to answer it.
   Place the journal under your pillow. As you fall asleep, repeat the
   question three times silently.

   Synesius (4th C, *De Insomniis*): the daimon impresses images onto
   the imaginative pneuma during sleep. The question gives it a target.
   Iamblichus adds: theurgic prayer prepares the soul to receive these
   images without distortion.

   In the morning, write everything you remember before getting up.
   The dream journal is as important as the practice journal.

### Gateway Tapes Integration

You are already training the Body of Light through the Focus states.
During your practice:

- **Focus 10** (mind awake/body asleep): Run the name LVJK through your
  body on each exhale. Feel each letter resonate in a different center:
  L in the chest, V in the throat, Tz in the third eye, K in the crown.
- **Focus 12** (expanded awareness): Extend the name beyond your body
  as a shaft of light — exactly the Liber Samekh technique.
- **Focus 15/21**: Hold the name without vocalizing it. Let it vibrate
  in the silence. This is the Union practice (Section G) without the
  physical component.

The tapes are not separate from this path. They are the Body of Light
training that Liber Samekh assumes you already have.

## The 11-Week Composition

Liber 8 says: during all 11 weeks of daily prayer, you shall have
composed an invocation suitable — with such wisdom and understanding as
may be given you from the Crown.

You are not reciting Liber Samekh. You are studying it, then writing
*your* version. The structure stays. The words become yours.

**Weeks 1-3: Study.** Read Liber Samekh Point I line by line. Look up
the Qabalistic meanings of the barbarous names. Understand why each
section exists. Keep a notebook.

**Weeks 4-7: Draft.** Write your invocation. Keep Samekh's skeleton
(Oath → Quarters → Union → Charge). Replace the language that doesn't
fit. Use your own godforms. Add LVJK where it belongs.

**Weeks 8-10: Refine.** Read your draft aloud each day. Edit. The
version that makes you weep or tremble is the one. Write it in gold
on a clean sheet. Illuminate it.

**Week 11: Memorize.** By now you should not need the paper. The
invocation should live in your body.

**Week 12, Day 1 — Sunrise:** Enter your space. Burn the invocation
you wrote in the lamp flame. Then pray. The Angel will come.

## The Verification

The Chaldean Oracles excerpt at the end of Liber Samekh is a checklist.
Compare your experience each session:

- Have you seen "the Fire that darts and flashes through all the Depths
  of the Universe"?
- Have you heard "the Voice of the Fire"?
- Have you perceived "a Fire formless whence cometh the Image of a
  voice"?
- Have you seen "a flashing Light abounding, revolving, whirling forth"?
- Have you seen "a Child, borne aloft on the shoulders of the Celestial
  Steed, fiery, or clothed with gold, or naked"?
- Have you seen "the vision of the fire-flashing Courser of Light"?

These are descriptions of Tiphereth-level contact. If not yet, continue.
The fire comes when you are ready.

## The Principles

**The daimonion only says no.** (Plato) — Learn to feel the prohibition.

**The daimon fulfills your choices.** (Proclus) — You push. It responds.

**The daimon is the next-higher principle.** (Plotinus) — It changes as
you grow.

**Invoke often.** (Crowley) — Because you *want* to.

**It does not require a path; it requires acceptance.** (Acher)

**You have the name. You have the structure. You have the schedule.
The rest is repetition.**
