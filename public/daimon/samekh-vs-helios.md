# Liber Samekh vs Helios Unbound — Comparison

| Dimension | Liber Samekh (Crowley) | Helios Unbound (Farrell) |
|-----------|----------------------|--------------------------|
| **Duration** | 11 months graduated | 7 months fixed |
| **Daily time** | Starts 1 session/day, ends 4+ sessions/day | 2.5 hrs/day, 3 sessions (fixed throughout) |
| **Structure** | One ritual repeated with increasing frequency | Progressive curriculum — different content each month |
| **Framework** | Thelemic / Qabalistic | Pagan / Hellenistic / Neoplatonic |
| **Astrology** | None required | Required — natal chart, planets, moon mansions, decans, Hellenistic Lots |
| **Mechanism** | Repetition of the SAME act deepens the groove | Escalating COMPLEXITY builds pressure |
| **Climax** | Section G — the Union (within the daily ritual) | 3-day retreat (Death → Ascension → Know Yourself) |
| **Retreat needed** | No (the 11th month is continual but not isolated) | Yes — final weekend, complete isolation |
| **Preparation** | Banishing + Body of Light | Purification + Confession + Vow (Month 1) |
| **Source of power** | Barbarous names + Shaft of Will + Resistance | Greek voces magicae + planetary spirits + "acceptable illusion" |
| **Psychological model** | "False Ego must die" (Section G) | "Lower Self must be hammered until it surrenders" |
| **Aftermath** | Angel frequents and trains the Adept | Integration takes months to years |
| **Portability** | Anywhere with a circle and the text | Requires temple/space, especially for retreat |
| **Cost** | Free (public domain) | Commercial book |
| **Philosophy** | "Invoke Often" — desire is the engine | "Acceptable Illusion" — consistent symbol-use is the engine |

## Which One for You?

**Liber Samekh** is better if:
- You want a single, portable ritual you can do anywhere
- You prefer depth through repetition over breadth of content
- You don't have astrological knowledge
- You can't isolate for a weekend retreat
- Thelemic/Qabalistic symbolism resonates

**Helios Unbound** is better if:
- You want a structured curriculum with progressive learning
- You have astrological knowledge or are willing to learn
- Pagan/Hellenistic symbolism resonates more than Thelemic
- You can commit 2.5 hrs daily and a weekend retreat
- You prefer building a complete magical system over mastering one ritual
