## Before You Start: The Skills You Need

Acher's path assumes you have three core skills. Practice these separately first.

### Skill 1: Meditation of the Void

> Acher references this repeatedly. It's the foundational meditative state.

**Practice (10 min daily):**
1. Sit in a dark, quiet space. Light a single candle.
2. Gaze at the flame. Let your mind settle.
3. Close your eyes. Hold the flame's afterimage in your mind's eye.
4. Let the afterimage dissolve. What remains is the void — the space between thoughts.
5. Rest in this space. When thoughts arise, gently return to the void.

### Skill 2: Magical Singing (Not Performance)

> You are not singing for others. You are making your body vibrate.

**Practice (5 min daily):**
1. Find a space where you won't be overheard.
2. Take a full breath. On the exhale, make a low sustained sound.
3. Place your hand on your chest. Feel the vibration.
4. Try vowels: AAAA, EEEE, IIII, OOOO, UUUU.
5. Map which vowels resonate in which body parts.
6. Sing the name **LVJK** — L-V-A-H-T-Z-K-E-L — one breath, let each letter resonate.

### Skill 3: Drawing Down a Planet (Visionary)

> Reaching out to a celestial body and establishing connection.

**Practice (5 min):**
1. Know where the planet is in the sky (use an app or the observatory).
2. Close your eyes. Visualize the planet's position relative to your body.
3. Reach toward it with your awareness — not your physical hand.
4. Feel the distance close. The planet is now present with you.
5. Notice any sensory impression: light, temperature, sound, shape.
6. Thank the planet and gently withdraw.

## Stage 1: Trust — Exercise Checklist

> Do these in order. Spend at least one week on each before moving on.

### Week 1: Stretching Your Polarities

```
□ Find a moment during the day (under 1 min)
□ Open left hand — completely relaxed, like putting a child to bed
□ Form right hand into a tight fist — increasing tension
□ Hold BOTH simultaneously (you are the bridge between them)
□ Reflect on the nature of the open hand: "This is a hand. It is an open hand."
□ Reflect on the nature of the fist: "This is a fist. It is a building tool. It is a killing tool."
□ You are NEITHER your open hand NOR your fist — you are between them
□ Release both. Notice how quickly they return to balance
□ Practise little and often — dozens of times throughout the day
```

### Week 2: Two Modes of Tratak

```
MODE 1 — FOCUSED (morning, 5 min):
  □ Dark, windless, undisturbed space
  □ Light a single candle (yellow if possible — LVJK's colour)
  □ Gaze at the flame without moving, blinking, or swallowing
  □ Close eyes — hold the negative imprint on your retina
  □ When it fades, open eyes and refresh
  □ Record observations in your journal

MODE 2 — PERIPHERAL (evening, 5 min):
  □ Two candles, one far left, one far right
  □ Rest gaze in the centre — hold both flames in peripheral vision
  □ Do not look at either directly
  □ Close eyes — hold the peripheral imprint
  □ When it fades, open eyes and refresh
  □ Note the difference between focused and peripheral perception
```

### Week 3: The Serpent and the Egg

```
PHASE 1 — BREAK (morning):
  □ One raw egg, one hard-boiled egg (start with raw for phase 1)
  □ Sit in a quiet place. Place both eggs before you
  □ Pick up the raw egg. Meditate:
    - What is the nature of an egg?
    - Are you the serpent? Will you incubate or break?
    - How many eggs have you broken unthinkingly?
  □ Take a deep breath. Throw the egg forcefully on the floor
  □ Sit with the broken egg. Meditate:
    - Where did the power to break it come from?
    - When have you used this destructive power before?
    - What do you see about the dangers of being you?
  □ Clean up

PHASE 2 — CARRY (entire day):
  □ Pick up the second egg (hard-boiled recommended for first time)
  □ Carry it in your hand ALL DAY
  □ Your goal: incubate it. Protect it. Bring it home safely
  □ When you can't hold it (driving, writing), place it next to you
  □ If asked: "I'm doing a mindfulness course"
  □ Notice: protecting life is MUCH harder than destroying it
  □ Journal: what did you learn about trust?
```

### Reflection After Stage 1

> Acher's question: **"Can I trust myself? Whatever power is accessible to
> me right now, can I trust myself to handle it wisely?"**

```
□ Write your answer in your journal
□ Do not move to Stage 2 until you can honestly answer "yes"
```

## Stage 2: Joy — Exercise Checklist

### Week 4-5: Igniting Hitlahavut

```
□ Find a remote place where you will NOT be overheard
□ Open a Bible/Psalms randomly. Let a line jump out.
  OR use a line that resonates with you from the correspondences
□ Commit the line to memory
□ Go to your remote space. Stand or sit with upright spine
□ Begin to sing the line. Not beautifully — just let your voice play
□ Let the words dissolve into pure sound, pure vibration
□ Track your progress:
  □ Can you feel your voice in different body parts?
  □ Can you map the five vowels to specific body centres?
  □ How long does the echo remain after you stop?
  □ Can you sing softly while maintaining impact?
  □ Can you direct your voice as a tool in vision?
□ For LVJK: Start by singing LVAHTZKEL on a single breath
```

### Week 6-7: Singing with the Planets

```
□ Choose a planet — start with SUN or MOON, NOT Jupiter or Saturn
□ For LVJK: Mercury is your native planet — do this one often
□ Know the planet's position (use an app or observatory)
□ Go to your remote space at the planet's day/hour
  Wednesday, Mercury hour = best for LVJK
□ Ground yourself in silence (tratak or void meditation)
□ Close eyes. Visualize the planet's position relative to your body
□ Reach out. Draw the planet down into your presence
□ Begin to sing to the planet. Let your voice be guided by its light
□ Observe:
  □ How does the planet respond to your voice?
  □ How does its response affect your body?
  □ Do you see patterns or currents between you and the planet?
  □ What differences between day hour and night hour?
□ WARNING: Do not sing for more than 15-20 minutes at first
□ Journal your observations for each planet
```

### Week 8: Sealing the Vessel with Joy

```
□ Morning hour of the Sun
□ Quiet space. Usual grounding
□ Reach out to the Sun. Draw it down — not too close
□ A good outcome: the Sun before you on the horizon, slightly larger than normal
□ Begin to sing for the Sun. Tune yourself to hear its answering sound
□ Concentrate your awareness on the MIDPOINT between you and the Sun
□ This midpoint is where you seal your vessel with joy
□ This joy is not an emotion — it is a force
□ Spend time at this midpoint. Return to it often
```

## Stage 3: Darkness — Exercise Checklist

> Do not rush this stage. Acher spent 4 weeks in a Saturn retreat AFTER
> 10 years of prior practice.

### Standing at the Threshold

```
PREREQUISITES:
  □ Lightproof and soundproof space (walk-in cupboard, or sleeping mask)
  □ Learn the incantation by heart:
    "Take what is worn. Devour what is waste. Peruse what is I.
     For I am not afraid, but born from your fire.
     We will meet again and again. In your crucible of ashes and life."
  □ Saturn's hour (Saturday morning or night)

THE RITE:
  □ Sit with a lit candle before you
  □ Ground yourself. Perform the Meditation of the Void
  □ When you are in the centre of the flame, lean forward
  □ Blow out the candle — see the flame vanish in vision
  □ Sit in darkness. Grow accustomed to it
  □ Reach toward the eastern horizon in vision
  □ Push deeper into the void until you find Saturn
  □ Watch the Old One who never speaks yet always listens
  □ Sing for Saturn. Sing the incantation
  □ When done, return to the flame in vision
  □ Return to your body. Sit in darkness awhile
  □ Write everything down

RULES:
  □ Do this no more than 13 times in your life
  □ Maximum once per week (Saturday)
  □ Spend weeks or YEARS on this exercise
  □ Do not force changes — let them manifest naturally
```

### Crossing the Threshold

```
PREREQUISITES:
  □ Your daimon's name: LVAHTZKEL (LVJK)
  □ Learn this PGM incantation by heart:
    "For you are I and I am you; your name is mine, and mine is yours.
     For I am your image, LVAHTZKEL."

THE RITE:
  □ Return to your lightproof, soundproof space
  □ Usual grounding. Tratak. Meditation of the Void
  □ When settled in the flame, extinguish the light in vision
  □ Float in darkness. Stay present in the absence
  □ Begin to sing the incantation of your holy daimon
  □ Do NOT expect your daimon to appear
  □ DO expect her/him to HEAR you
  □ Sing in devotion. Sing toward the presence
  □ "For the first time in your life, you are no longer singing to
     the mirror. You have turned around and started singing to the real thing."
  □ Keep singing. You will know what to do.
```

## Stage 4: Encounter — Systasis Ritual Checklist

> Attempt only after completing Stages 1-3.
> Repeat at each crescent moon for at least 6 months.

### Materials Preparation

```
□ 6 pebbles, egg-sized, never used magically
□ 2 fresh organic raw eggs
□ Myrrh ink and fine brush
□ White candle and matches
□ Commit the Helios conjuration to memory:
  "Welcome, Helios, for you are the one who has established yourself
   in invisible light over the holy firmament ORKORĒTHARA. You are the
   father of the reborn Aion ZARACHTHŌ; you are the father of the
   inaccessible Nature Thortchophanō; you are the one who has in yourself
   the mixture of universal nature and who begot the five wandering stars..."
□ Write the Helios name on both eggs (coil text around shell):
  "AŌ SATHREN ABRASAX IAŌAI AEŌ ĒŌA ŌAĒ IAO IĒO EY IE IAŌAI"
```

### The Rite — Five Stages

```
STAGE 1 — SKINNING (5 min):
  □ Light the candle. Place 3 stones left, 3 stones right. Eggs behind you
  □ Ground yourself. Meditation of the Void
  □ Pick up first stone. Roll between palms. In vision, draw off a layer of skin
  □ "Welcome, Tyche, spirit of fate." → place before you
  □ Second stone: "Welcome, daimon of this place."
  □ Third stone: "Welcome, daimon of the present hour."
  □ Fourth stone: "Welcome, daimon of the present day."
  □ Fifth stone: "Welcome, daimon of time and every day."
  □ Sixth stone: "Welcome, daimon of the All-embracing, that is, earth and heaven."
  □ Sit in the presence of the six daimons

STAGE 2 — PURIFYING (5 min):
  □ Take the first egg. Stroke it over your entire body like a sponge
  □ Lick off the divine name written on it
  □ Crack the egg. Empty it on the ground between you and the stones
  □ Observe your body in vision — skinned and purified

STAGE 3 — CHARGING (10 min):
  □ Take the second egg. Centre yourself in the void
  □ Reach out to the Sun. Draw close (but not too close)
  □ Pull cosmic fire into the egg until it glows like an ember
  □ Raise your right hand, elbow supported by left
  □ Speak the Helios conjuration 7 times, from memory
  □ Know these words. They are fire.

STAGE 4 — INFLAMING (immediate):
  □ Crack the egg. Swallow its contents raw
  □ Follow the cosmic fire in vision as it enters your body
  □ Do NOT interfere. Do NOT touch it with imagination
  □ Just witness

STAGE 5 — COMMUNION:
  □ Call for your holy daimon — sing LVAHTZKEL
  □ Visualize it approach from BEHIND you
  □ "It is vast and beautiful."
  □ "It is now touching you on your shoulders."
  □ Everything else will transpire naturally
  □ You are now in holy communion

AFTER:
  □ Rest. Do not speak for an hour
  □ Write down everything you experienced
  □ Drink water. Eat something grounding
```
