# Helios Unbound — Commentary

> Nick Farrell, *Helios Unbound: Pagan Theurgy to Connect to Your Higher Genius* (2020)
> A 7-month Pagan theurgy programme to meet the Higher Genius, born from the author's
> failed Abramelin attempt and subsequent 17 years of development.

---

## Origin Story

Farrell attempted the Abramelin in 2003 in Bulgaria. He found the Christian prayers
hollow — "The more I heard myself talking to this abstract Christian god, the less
I liked it." He aborted in month 3 after his house was burgled and tools stolen.

What he learned: the intense focused effort worked. The problem was the framework,
not the structure. Over a weekend, he designed a Pagan alternative — 7 months
instead of 6, going upward through Moon, planets, stars, and finally the Higher
Genius. The original draft sat in a file called "oldlinuxbackup2003" for 17 years.
He rediscovered it in 2017, updated it, and published as Helios Unbound.

## Key Principles

### 1. The "Acceptable Illusion"

> "The process works by providing consistent and powerful symbols which remind the
> person that their Higher Genius is within them. This creates an 'acceptable illusion'
> that the magician is ascending towards their Higher Genius as it simultaneously
> advances towards them."

The mechanics: you are hammering your Lower Self until it accepts the truth of the
divine spark within. The symbols are the hammer. You don't need to "believe" in
them — you need to USE them consistently.

### 2. Three Sessions Daily, 2.5 Hours Total

Farrell is brutally honest about the time commitment:
- Waking session: ~30 min
- Midday session: ~30 min
- Main session (night): ~1 hour
- **Total: 2.5 hours/day for 7 months**

This is comparable to Abramelin's prayer load but structured differently.

### 3. The Lower Self Will Rebel

> "The process is psychologically difficult and is opposed at all stages by the
> Lower Self, which considers its 'learnt by experience' way safer. Its rebellion
> will involve opposition to the imposed discipline to all-out psychological revolt
> through fear and even hallucinations."

This is not poetic language. Farrell means actual psychological backlash — fear,
doubt, external chaos, relationships fraying. The Lower Self fights for survival.

### 4. Prima Materia — "Act as if"

A core technique: you rehearse what it feels like to have ALREADY united with
your Higher Genius. You don't pray FOR it — you perform FROM it. This is the
"acceptable illusion" made operational.

### 5. Three Invocations Daily

You invoke the Higher Genius three times a day for seven months. By the time
you reach the retreat, the pattern is so deeply embedded that the final contact
is the natural culmination of a rhythm, not a desperate grab.

## The 3-Day Retreat

The climax follows a classic initiatory death-rebirth arc:

- **Day 1 — Death & Destruction:** Invocation of Serapis (the dying god). Journey
  into the Egyptian underworld. The sacred candle is lit — a physical symbol of
  the Higher Genius that burns throughout.

- **Day 2 — Ascension & Immortalisation:** Invoke higher angelic forces as a
  ladder. Meet the forces behind the seven rays of Helios' crown. Vision of your
  immortal aspect — the Hero within.

- **Day 3 — Know Yourself:** The barrier between you and your Higher Genius is
  deliberately broken down. Direct communication begins. Chant until the
  experience happens.

> Farrell's own experience: on Day 2 he "experienced Mithras-Aion and understood
> what it was all about — at least for me." Day 3 was about building that experience
> into life.

## Lessons Learned

**From Farrell's failed Abramelin:** The Christian framework didn't work for him,
but the STRUCTURE did. He kept the intensity, replaced the theology. This validates
the approach of adapting these operations to your actual belief system rather than
forcing yourself into an incompatible framework.

**From the 17-year gap:** The system kept working on him even when he wasn't
actively using it. The 2003 outline resurfaced in 2017 and "fit my current
thinking tightly." The ideas were still alive, still developing.

**From the testing phase:** People who tried the system "seemed to get the required
results." The key was that the 7-month build-up created enough pressure in the
Sphere of Sensation to force psychological issues to the surface where they could
be dealt with — not avoided.

**From the retreat:** The most intense experiences happen when you are completely
cut off and focused. Farrell's own contact with Mithras-Aion occurred on Day 2
of isolation, not during the 7 months of preparation. The preparation made the
retreat possible; the retreat made the contact happen.
