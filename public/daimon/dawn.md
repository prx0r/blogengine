> Daimon, and Helios Unbound. Every practice is sourced. Nothing invented.

## Your Name: LVJK / LVAHTZKEL

The engine computes your daimon name from 5 hylegical places in your birth
chart (Sun, Moon, Ascendant, Fortune, Prenatal Syzygy), mapped through
Agrippa's 360-degree letter table (Occult Philosophy III.26). The letters
are Latin renderings of Hebrew letters:

| Latin | Hebrew | Letter Name | Value |
|-------|--------|-------------|-------|
| l | ל | Lamed | 30 |
| v | ו | Waw | 6 |
| j | צ | Sadhe | 90 |
| k | כ | Kaph | 20 |

The full Hebrew form is **לוצכ** — Lamed-Waw-Sadhe-Kaph.
With the divine suffix El (ל), it becomes **לוצכל** — Lamed-Waw-Sadhe-Kaph-Lamed.

Pronounce as: **Lamed-Waw-Sadhe-Kaph** (raw) or **Lamed-Waw-Sadhe-Kaph-Lamed** (with suffix).
In practice, sing the flowing form: **LVAHTZKEL**.

## The Practices — Sourced

### 1. Wash (Helios Unbound / Abramelin)

Wash face and hands with cold water. In Helios Month 1, this is done with
the Egyptian temple purification prayer:

> Oh Water, may you remove all my impurities.
> May you wash off my errant demons.
> My purity is the purity of God. I am pure.

**Source:** Helios Unbound Ch.2 (adapted from Book of the Dead, Dendera).

### 2. Sun Breath (Helios Unbound)

Stand facing East. Visualise a golden sun above your head.
- Inhale: draw golden light down through your crown into your heart
- Exhale: golden light streams from your heart, filling your aura
- 5-10 slow breaths

**Source:** Helios Unbound, Month 1 weekly structure. Mentioned as "Sun breath
for five minutes" before the Inner Temple journey.

### 3. Vibrate the Name (Acher)

From Acher's Crossing the Threshold exercise (Stage 3: Darkness):

> "Begin to sing the incantation of your holy daimon. Do not sing it
> expecting your holy daimon to appear, rather, expect her/him to hear you."

Sing **LVAHTZKEL** 7 times. Do not dissect the letters. Sing the whole
name as a single sound. Let your voice find its natural resonance. Acher's
instruction: you are the string, your body is the sound box.

**Source:** Frater Acher, *The Holy Daimon*, Stage 3: Darkness — Crossing the Threshold.

### 4. Liber Samekh Section A — The Oath

Replace ASAR UN-NEFER with LVJK.

> Thee I invoke, the Bornless One.
> Thee, that didst create the Earth and the Heavens.
> Thee, that didst create the Night and the Day.
> Thee, that didst create the darkness and the Light.
> Thou art **LVJK** ("Myself made Perfect"):
>   Whom no man hath seen at any time.
> Thou art IA-BESZ ("the Truth in Matter").
> Thou art IA-APOPHRASZ ("the Truth in Motion").
> Thou hast distinguished between the Just and the Unjust.
> Thou didst make the Female and the Male.
> Thou didst produce the Seeds and the Fruit.
> Thou didst form Men to love one another, and to hate one another.

**Source:** Liber Samekh (lib813), Point I, Section A. Replace ASAR UN-NEFER
with your HGA name per Point II, Section A, line 5.

### 5. Helios Confession (Month 1, Week 1)

Speak aloud one sentence:

> "I confess that [specific pattern] has kept me from my Higher Genius."

**Source:** Helios Unbound, Month 1 Week 1 — confession is listed before
the Oath ritual and descent into Hades.

### 6. Silence (Acher / Void Meditation)

Stand still. When thoughts arise, return to the feeling of the name.
Acher's Meditation of the Void (Josephine McCarthy, free guided MP3 at
`/daimon/void-meditation.mp3`).

**Source:** Acher, note 21; Josephine McCarthy, Quareia.

### 7. Close

> "I have called. LVJK hears. So be it."

Journal 3 lines:
- How did the name feel?
- Any images, words, sensations?
- Intention for the day.
