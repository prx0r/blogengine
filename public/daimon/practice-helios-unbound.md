# Helios Unbound — Practice Guide for Lvahtzkel

> Full practice text from Nick Farrell, *Helios Unbound: Pagan Theurgy to
> Connect to Your Higher Genius* (2020). A 7-month Pagan programme for
> achieving Knowledge and Conversation of the Higher Genius / HGA.
> Integrated with our engine for astrological layers.

---

## The Core Philosophy: "Acceptable Illusion"

> "This process creates an 'acceptable illusion' that the magician is ascending
> towards their Higher Genius as it simultaneously advances towards them. The
> reality is that over an intense period, you are hammering your Lower Self
> until it accepts the truth of the divine spark within."

You do not need to "believe" in the gods, the planetary spirits, or the
Higher Genius as literal entities. You need to USE the symbols consistently.
The Lower Self does not distinguish between a "real" spiritual experience
and a powerfully enacted symbolic one — it responds to both. This is the
same principle behind our astrological engine: the ActivationPacket is pure
signal; the meaning is layered on top by tradition.

## The Daily Structure (All 7 Months)

Three sessions daily, every day, without fail:

| Session | When | Duration | Core Content |
|---------|------|----------|-------------|
| **Waking** | Within 15 min of rising | ~30 min | Solar invocation, Sun breath, journey to Inner Temple |
| **Midday** | Around noon | ~30 min | Solar invocation, Prima Materia exercise |
| **Night** | Evening (main session) | ~1 hour | Solar invocation, month's ritual work, Prima Materia, meditation |

**Total: ~2.5 hours/day. Every day. For 7 months.**

If you miss a day, the momentum breaks. Farrell is explicit: the power of
the system depends on NEVER missing a session. The Lower Self will find
every excuse — work, family, illness, mood. Push through.

## The Core Practices (Used Every Month)

### 1. Solar Invocations (Three Times Daily)

Each session begins with an invocation to the Sun appropriate to the time
of day. These invoke Helios as the bridge between the material and divine.
The specific invocations are given in the book and adapted per month.

### 2. Sun Breath (5 min)

A breathing exercise that solarises the Sphere of Sensation:
1. Stand or sit upright. Visualise a golden sun above your head.
2. Inhale: draw golden light down through your crown into your heart.
3. Hold: let the light circulate through your body.
4. Exhale: golden light streams from your heart, filling your aura.
5. Repeat for 5 minutes.

### 3. Journey to the Inner Temple

A guided visualisation to an inner temple space where the monthly work
is conducted. The temple is built and furnished over the 7 months,
becoming more detailed and numinous as you progress.

### 4. Prima Materia Exercise (At Least Once Daily)

The foundational alchemical practice. Done whenever possible:

**Stage 1 (Weeks 1-4):**
- Sit with back straight. Eyes open. Be fully aware of your body.
- Breathe slowly, shifting awareness deeper into your physical frame.
- Feel every cell, your blood moving, your heart beating.
- Sense your physical environment with your entire body.
- Vibrate **IAO** three times (silently if in public).

**Stage 2 (After ~1 month of practice):**
- Same as Stage 1, but you will notice gold flecks or white light
  moving in your body and environment.
- Do NOT focus on these. Ignore them. They are signs the Prima
  Materia is being transformed by your attention.
- Continue the practice. The divine self begins manifesting in matter.

## Month-by-Month Structure

### Month 1: Purification & Consecration

**Theme:** Breaking down what is wrong with your life. Confession of faults.
Redirecting misused power toward the Higher Genius.

**Week 1:** LBRP, Sun breath, Inner Temple journey, Sun invocation,
confession, oath ritual, descent into Hades ritual, binding ritual,
"spell to become a magician."

**Week 2:** Add purification ritual (assume godform of Nephthys).
Purify the Sphere of Sensation.

**Week 3:** Add consecration ritual (assume godform of Isis).
Consecrate the Sphere of Sensation.

**Week 4:** Add opening of the mouth ritual. The Sphere of Sensation
is now a fit vehicle for the Higher Genius.

### Month 2: The Elements

**Theme:** Balance the four elements within the Sphere of Sensation.

**Week 1 — Earth:** Black candle. Taurus + Earth triangle. Banishing.
Elemental invocation. Assume godform of Hera.

**Week 2 — Water:** Blue candle. Scorpio + Water triangle. Banishing.
Elemental invocation. Assume godform of Persephone.

**Week 3 — Air:** Yellow candle. Aquarius + Air triangle. Banishing.
Elemental invocation. Assume godform of Zeus.

**Week 4 — Fire:** Red candle. Leo + Fire triangle. Banishing.
Elemental invocation. Assume godform of Hades.

Final day: Spirit candle (white). Balance all four elements.

### Month 3: Sacred Language

**Theme:** Ancient Greek and voces magicae. The sacred alphabet as a
technology for contacting the divine. Adapted Rosicrucian technique to
draw the Higher Genius downward.

Each week works with a set of Greek letters, their numerical values,
and their correspondences. You chant sequences of vowels to create
vibrational patterns that align the soul with the Intelligible realm.

### Month 4: The Seven (Planets)

**Theme:** Using your natal chart, work with the seven planetary powers.
Each planet gets 4 days of focused work.

**For each of the 7 planets (4 days each = 28 days):**
1. Take an appropriately coloured candle. Inscribe with planetary sigils.
2. Invoke the planetary spirit (use the specific names provided).
3. Say the Orphic Hymn for that planet.
4. Meditate on how this planet manifests in your chart and life.
5. The goal is not to be a victim of planetary forces, but to co-operate
   with them consciously.

**Integrated with our engine:** The observatory provides your natal
planetary positions, current transits, and planetary hours — exactly
what this month requires.

### Month 5: Moon Magic & the Demon of the Soul

**Theme:** The Moon as the gatekeeper of the sublunary realm. The 28
lunar mansions and their spirits (the "Demons of the Soul").

Each of the 28 nights invokes a specific lunar mansion spirit using
a named candle and the Hekate invocations provided. The Moon is the
closest celestial power — it directly controls nature, emotion, and
the gateway between material and spiritual.

**Integrated with our engine:** We compute moon phase and sign already.
The 28 lunar mansions can be calculated from the moon's position.

### Month 6: The Mysteries of the 12 (Zodiac & Decans)

**Theme:** The twelve zodiacal signs, the decans, and the Hellenistic
Lots. The forces that shape your destiny.

Place your natal chart on the altar. Arrange 12 tarot cards in a circle
matching your chart. Each session invokes a zodiacal spirit and its
decans, working through all 12 over the month.

**Integrated with our engine:** Full natal chart computation, decan
calculation, and Hellenistic Lots (Fortune, Spirit, Eros, Necessity,
Courage, Victory, Nemesis) are all already in our engine.

### Month 7: Mysteries of the Higher Genius

**Theme:** The final month. Higher angelic forces, the forces of Divine
Love. Builds to the 3-day retreat.

The first 3 weeks intensify the daily work, invoking abstract Pagan
angels and preparing the candidate for the final retreat.

## The 3-Day Retreat

### Day 1 — Death (Friday)

Theme: Spiritual death so that you can awaken to new life.

- **Before dawn:** Wash. Recite Egyptian temple purification prayer.
- **The Speech Before the Door:** Address Hermanubis (doorkeeper).
  "Make way for me so that I can enter the holiest of places, for I am
  one of you, I am Serapis, and I have entered the underworld and risen
  with the Sun."
- **The First Rite:** Kindle the sacred candle (inscribed with your name
  and the Helios symbols). This candle burns throughout the entire
  retreat as a beacon (Pharos) for the Higher Genius.
- **Morning:** Prayer to Helios. Invocation of the Higher Genius.
- **Day:** Sequence of rituals focused on death of the old self.
  Journey into the Egyptian underworld. Meet the spirits there.
  Witness your own Higher Genius awakening.
- **Night:** Continue invocations. Let the candle burn.

### Day 2 — Ascension (Saturday)

Theme: Immortalisation. Ascending to meet the divine.

- **Morning:** Invoke higher Pagan angelic forces. They provide a
  ladder to ascend to more rarefied states.
- **Sequence 1:** Meet the spiritual forces behind all gods and angels
  — the seven rays on Helios' crown.
- **Sequence 2:** Ascension through the levels. Vision of your Higher
  Genius in a form you can comprehend.
- **Sequence 3:** Meet the Fates. Vision of your immortal aspect.
  See the Hero within.
- **Farrell's experience:** On Day 2 he "experienced Mithras-Aion and
  understood what it was all about."

### Day 3 — Know Yourself (Sunday)

Theme: The final barrier between you and your Higher Genius is broken.

- **Morning:** Final invocations.
- **The Breaking:** A ritual designed to break down the barriers between
  the magician and the Higher Genius.
- **First sequence:** Ritual invocation to the Higher Genius.
- **Second sequence:** Chant until the experience happens.
  Direct communication begins.
- **After the contact:** Integration. The Higher Genius begins to work
  through you.

## Integration (After the Retreat)

Farrell: "Afterwards, it took another rewrite to make sure those
experiences were duplicated for those who try them."

The period after the retreat is as important as the preparation. The
Higher Genius will begin to work in your life — your task is to pay
attention, to follow the guidance, and to continue the relationship
through regular practice (though less intense than the 7 months).
