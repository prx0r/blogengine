### Key Unresolved Questions from the Session
1. **Recognition vs Transformation** — irreducible disagreement between Tantraloka (recognition) and Tsongkhapa (transformation). Is liberation a change in knowledge or a change in being?
2. **Technique vs Grace** — does practice CAUSE realization or EXPRESS grace? Tantraloka says grace is the sole cause; Tsongkhapa says practice is the efficient cause.
3. **The navigator problem** — what individuates consciousness across lives? Three competing answers: Tantraloka's navātman, Tsongkhapa's wind-mind continuum, daimon path's HGA as attractor.
4. **Is Śiva the same as Śūnyatā?** — fullness vs emptiness. Abhinavagupta says consciousness is positive (pūrṇa). Nāgārjuna says emptiness is the absence of inherent existence. Not the same.
5. **Is consciousness computable?** — Penrose says no (non-computable). Zenil's volume splits. Tantraloka: the 36 tattvas are computable; the Anākhyā (38th) is not. Both sides correct at different depths.
6. **The 38th principle** — can the enabling condition be positively described? The Tantraloka says yes (śakti as anākhyā). The Madhyamaka says no (śūnyatā is not a thing).
