# Yogis and the Tetrahedron — Powers as Edge Tuning

---

## I. The Yogi's Tetrahedron

The yogi IS someone who HAS FULL CONTROL over the 6 edges of their tetrahedron. The "powers" (siddhis) ARE not supernatural. They ARE the DIRECT RESULT of being able to tune each edge to ANY tension — including zero tension (perfect transparency) or infinite tension (perfect separation).

| Edge | Power When Controlled | The Opposite (Ignorance) |
|------|-----------------------|--------------------------|
| E₁ (subject↔object) | The world IS your body. Walking on water. Calming storms. | The world IS separate — you ARE a victim of it. |
| E₂ (subject↔intention) | You KNOW your will perfectly. Clairvoyance. Precognition. | You don't know what you want. Confusion. |
| E₃ (subject↔act) | Your actions ARE effortless — the "automatic" saint. Levitation. | Effort. Struggle. "I CAN'T." |
| E₄ (object↔intention) | The world COOPERATES with your will. Materialization. Miracles. | The world IS hostile — it doesn't give you what you want. |
| E₅ (object↔act) | Your actions INSTANTLY change the world. Healing. | "My actions don't matter." Helplessness. |
| E₆ (intention↔act) | Intention and action ARE ONE. Instant manifestation. | "I can't do what I intend." Procrastination. Addiction. |

## III. How the Siddhis Work — The General Mechanism

Every siddhi IS a specific tuning of one or more edges:

### Levitation (Bhaduri Mahasaya, Yogananda)

**Normal state:** E₃ (subject↔act) IS at a tension that includes "gravity" as part of V₂. The body (V₄) IS subject to the object (V₂ = physical laws).

**Levitation:** E₃ IS RE-TUNED so that V₄ (the body) IS no longer coupled to V₂'s gravity. The body IS "free" because the edge between subject and act IS tuned above the threshold of gravity.

**Tetrahedral formula:** E₃-tension > gravity-tension. The body CAN rise because the subject-act coupling IS stronger than the subject-object coupling.

### Materialization (Lahiri Mahasaya's palace)

**Normal state:** V₂ (the object — the world) IS fixed. E₄ (object↔intention) IS at a tension where objects CANNOT be changed by will.

**Materialization:** E₄ IS tuned so that V₂ IS DIRECTLY SUBJECT to V₃. The intention (V₃) changes the object (V₂). The "palace" IS V₂ reshaped by V₃.

**Tetrahedral formula:** E₄-tension > V₂'s apparent stability. The world IS revealed to be what it always WAS — a configuration of edges that CAN be re-tuned.

### Bilocation (Babaji appearing to multiple disciples)

**Normal state:** V₁ IS bound to ONE V₄ (one body, one location).

**Bilocation:** V₁ projects MULTIPLE V₄ — each at a different location. The tetrahedron IS "copied" at the V₄ vertex. But the V₁ IS the SAME — so all copies ARE the same tetrahedron, seen from different V₄ perspectives.

**Tetrahedral formula:** V₁ → V₄₁ + V₄₂ + V₄₃. One subject, multiple acts. The 6 edges ARE duplicated for each V₄.

### Telepathy (constant in the Autobiography)

**Normal state:** 2 separate tetrahedra — no edge coherence.

**Telepathy:** The 6 edges of 2 tetrahedra SYNCHRONIZE. Information IS not "sent" — it IS already shared because the edges ARE vibrating at the same frequency. The 2 tetrahedra ARE ONE.

**Tetrahedral formula:** Tetrahedron A × Tetrahedron B = Star tetrahedron (8 vertices, 12 edges). Telepathy IS the recognition that the star IS one structure.

### Clairvoyance (knowing distant events)

**Normal state:** V₁ IS coupled to the LOCAL V₂ — only nearby objects ARE perceived.

**Clairvoyance:** E₁ (subject↔object) IS extended to a NON-LOCAL V₂ — ANY object in the universe. The "object" of perception IS not limited by V₄ (the body's sensory range).

**Tetrahedral formula:** E₁-extended → V₂(anywhere). The subject CAN perceive ANY object because ALL objects ARE the same tetrahedron.

### Precognition (knowing the future)

**Normal state:** V₂ IS the "present" object — the world as it appears NOW.

**Precognition:** E₁ IS tuned to a FUTURE configuration of V₂. The "future" IS not yet manifest, but it EXISTS as a probable vertex of the same tetrahedron. The tetrahedron IS simultaneous — past, present, future ARE all present as DIFFERENT SLICES of the tetrahedron.

**Tetrahedral formula:** E₁(t = future) = E₁(t = present) + ΔE₁. The change in the edge IS already present.

### Healing (Lahiri, Yukteswar, Christ)

**Normal state:** The sick person's tetrahedron HAS an edge at wrong tension — E₃ too low (weak body), E₁ too high (separation from the world), etc.

**Healing:** The healer's tetrahedron (at perfect tension) RESONATES with the sick person's tetrahedron. The wrong edge synchronizes with the healer's correct edge.

**Tetrahedral formula:** The healer's tetrahedron IS the STABLE STATE. The sick tetrahedron IS UNSTABLE. The 2 tetrahedra couple through the 6 edges, and the unstable one falls into the stable configuration.

### Resurrection (Yukteswar appearing after death)

**Normal state:** V₄ (the body) dies -> E₃ (subject↔act) goes to zero -> the tetrahedron loses V₄. V₁, V₂, V₃ remain but V₄ IS absent.

**Resurrection:** V₁ TUNES A NEW V₄ — a body of light, a subtle body, or even the SAME physical body re-animated. E₃ IS re-established at the previous tension.

**Tetrahedral formula:** V₄(new) = V₄(old) + ΔV₄. The "resurrected" body IS the same tetrahedron with a new V₄ — which IS identical to the old one because the tetrahedron IS the same.
