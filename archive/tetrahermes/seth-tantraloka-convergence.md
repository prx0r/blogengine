## I. Time and Reincarnation Through the Tetrahedron

> *"Time as you experience it IS an illusion caused by your physical senses. Everything in the universe exists at one time simultaneously. The past, present, and future only appear to those who exist within three-dimensional reality."*

### Reincarnation in the Timeless View

Seth: *"Reincarnation IS not linear. You live all your lives at once. The 'past life' IS not behind you — it IS a different focus of the same tetrahedron."*

In tetrahedral terms: the series I₁ → I₂ → I₃ → ... → Iₙ IS not a line. It IS a NESTING. Each life IS a tetrahedron. The lives ARE nested inside each other like Russian dolls. The "future life" IS not in the future — it IS a different RESOLUTION of the same tetrahedron you ARE living NOW.

| Life | Tetrahedral Level | What's Being Worked On |
|------|------------------|----------------------|
| Past life 1 | The base vertex — Earth | Physical survival, the body |

| Current life | The subject vertex — Śiva | Identity, awareness, self-knowledge |
| Future life | The object vertex — Īśvara | The world, the field of all possibilities |
| All lives | The FULL tetrahedron | All 4 vertices simultaneously — liberation |

The "past life" IS not behind you. It IS the BASE of the tetrahedron you ARE NOW. The "future life" IS not ahead of you. It IS the APEX — the same tetrahedron, seen from above.

### "Reality Transversing"

The term you're thinking of IS likely "Reality Transurfing" by Vadim Zeland — a modern Russian system about "transurfing" through probabilities. It IS a simplified version of the same tetrahedron concept: you choose which reality to "transurf" into by modulating your attention and intention. The "transurfing" IS the subject vertex (V₁) selecting which region of the object vertex (V₂ — the space of all probabilities) to experience. Seth calls this "choosing among probable realities."
