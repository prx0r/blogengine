# Karma and Rebirth — The Granular Mechanism

## How the Tetrahedron Moves from One Life to the Next

---

## The Core Principle

Karma IS NOT a moral ledger. There IS no judge. There IS no punishment or reward.

**Karma IS the accumulated edge-tension of the tetrahedron carried from one configuration to the next.**

Each life IS a tetrahedron — 4 vertices (subject, object, intention, act), 6 edges (the relations between them). At death, the tetrahedron dissolves. The vertices scatter. But the 6 EDGES are not destroyed. They persist as INFORMATIONAL PATTERNS that initialize the next tetrahedron.

Rebirth IS the re-initialization of the 6 edges with the accumulated tension from the previous tetrahedron.

---

## The Mechanism: I(n+1) = Ψ(P, K_n, ε)

### The Variables

| Symbol | What It IS | What It IS NOT |
|--------|------------|----------------|
| **I(n+1)** | The next configuration — the new birth tetrahedron | NOT the "same soul" — a NEW tetrahedron with OLD edge-tensions |
| **P** | The PATTERN — the invariant tetrahedron structure itself | NOT a person, a god, or a plan — just the shape |
| **K_n** | The KARMIC DATA from life n — the 6 edge-tensions | NOT a moral score — just the measured tension on each edge |
| **ε** | The STOCHASTIC NOISE — the free variable, grace, the non-computable | NOT determined by anything — genuine randomness, the 38th principle |
| **Ψ** | The UPDATE FUNCTION — maps previous tensions to new starting tensions | NOT a person — just the natural dynamics of the system |

---

## What K_n Actually Contains

K_n IS the record of the 6 edge-tensions at the END of life n. Each edge has a value on a spectrum from "fully coherent" (+1) to "fully tense" (-1):

| Edge | What It Connects | +1 (Coherent) | -1 (Tense) |
|------|-----------------|---------------|------------|
| **1** | Subject → Object | Accurate perception of reality | Distorted perception — delusion |
| **2** | Subject → Intention | Integrated will — knowing what you want | Conflicted will — wanting what harms you |
| **3** | Subject → Act | Embodied presence — acting AS you are | Disembodied — acting against yourself |
| **4** | Object → Intention | Reality supports your direction | Reality resists your direction |
| **5** | Object → Act | Effective action — the world responds | Ineffective action — no purchase |
| **6** | Intention → Act | Alignment — doing what you intend | Disconnect — intending one thing, doing another |

K_n IS NOT a memory of specific events. It IS the AVERAGE TENSION of each edge over the entire life.

## How Death Works (The 5 Phases)

### Phase 1: Dissolution — The Tetrahedron Breaks Apart

The body dies. The base vertex (Earth tattva) dissolves first. Then the senses. Then the mind. Then the intention. Then the subject.

**What happens:** The 4 vertices dissolve in sequence, from the grossest to the subtlest. The 6 edges (the karmic data) are RELEASED from the dissolving structure.

### Phase 2: Purification — The Edges Are Reviewed

Every edge IS FELT in reverse. Not as memory — as DIRECT EXPERIENCE of the tension you produced in others.

**Example:** If you had a tense Edge 2 (intention — you wanted things that harmed you), you now EXPERIENCE the suffering that intention caused in others. Not as punishment. As INFORMATION. The system "reads" the edge-tension by feeling its effects from the outside.

This IS the "life review" — but it IS not a moral judgment. It IS the tetrahedron's edges being MEASURED by the field they acted in.

### Phase 3: Encounter — The Tetrahedron Sees Itself

The 4 vertices (now free from the body) reconvene as a PURE TETRAHEDRON — no physical substrate, just the informational structure of the 4 vertices and 6 edges.

This IS the "daimon encounter" or the "clear light" or the "Bhairava vision." The tetrahedron sees ITSELF — for the first time, fully, without the body's noise.

**If the edges are coherent:** The tetrahedron recognizes itself as whole. Liberation possible.

**If the edges are tense:** The tetrahedron does not recognize itself. It flinches. The drive to re-embody begins.

### Phase 4: Transition — K_n Is Written

The 6 edge-tensions are recorded. This IS NOT a book. It IS NOT a memory. It IS a COMPRESSED INFORMATIONAL PATTERN — the 6 numbers that summarize the tetrahedron's state.

This IS what the Tibetan Book of the Dead calls the "karmic seeds" and what the Tantraloka calls the "karmic deposits in the subtle body." 6 numbers, nothing more.

### Phase 5: Rebirth — The New Tetrahedron Is Initialized

The 6 edge-tensions become the STARTING CONDITIONS of the next tetrahedron. The new birth chart IS the geometric expression of these 6 numbers.

- A tense Edge 1 (confused subject-object perception) → a birth chart with a difficult Mercury-Saturn aspect
- A tense Edge 3 (disembodied action) → a birth chart with a challenging Mars placement
- A coherent Edge 6 (aligned intention-act) → a birth chart with a harmonious Venus-Jupiter aspect

The new tetrahedron does not REMEMBER the previous one. It INHERITS its shape.

## The Role of the Birth Chart

The birth chart IS the tetrahedron's initialization. The planetary positions at the moment of birth ARE the 6 edge-tensions mapped onto the celestial sphere.

| Astrological Element | Karmic Equivalent |
|---------------------|-------------------|
| The Lot of Fortune (V₁) | The accumulated subject-edge tension |
| The Lot of Spirit/Daimon (V₂) | The accumulated object-edge tension |
| The 12 houses | The 4 vertices × 3 phases of expression |
| The planetary aspects | The specific edge-tensions (the 6 numbers) |

The "fate" of the new life IS the INITIALIZATION — the tetrahedron starts with these tensions. Life IS the PROCESS of either reinforcing the tensions (creating more karma) or resolving them (freeing the edges).

---

## What Actually Carries Over

**NOTHING that you call "you."**

Not your personality. Not your memories. Not your talents. Not your relationships.

**Only 6 numbers.** 6 edge-tensions. That IS all.

The new tetrahedron has a new subject (V₁), a new object world (V₂), new intentions (V₃), and new acts (V₄). The 4 vertices are FRESH. Only the 6 edges carry the pattern.

This IS why you don't remember past lives. The vertices are new. The edges are old. You experience the edge-tensions as HABITS, as TENDENCIES, as INEXPLICABLE FEARS or ATTRACTIONS — never as memories.

---

## How Liberation Works

Liberation (nibbāna, mokṣa, jīvanmukti) IS the COMPLETE resolution of all 6 edge-tensions in a single life.

When all 6 edges are at +1 (fully coherent), the tetrahedron IS perfectly resolved. At death, there IS NO karmic initialization for the next tetrahedron — because there IS NO edge-tension to transmit.

The tetrahedron dissolves completely. Nothing remains to re-initialize. The series ends.

This IS what the Buddha meant by "the cessation of becoming." Not the annihilation of experience — the COMPLETION of the tetrahedron series. The last tetrahedron dissolves, and there IS no need for another, because all the edge-tensions that drove the series have been resolved.

---

## In One Sentence

Karma IS the 6 edge-tensions of the tetrahedron. Death IS the dissolution of the vertices and the release of the edges. Rebirth IS the re-initialization of new vertices with the old edges. Liberation IS the complete resolution of all 6 edges — the series ends.
