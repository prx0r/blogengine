# Karma — The Granular Mechanism Explained Again, More Carefully

## What Edge-Tensions Actually Are, How the Invariant Structure Gets Modified, and What Steiner, the Buddha, and the Hindus Say

---

## I. The Question

If the tetrahedron IS invariant — always 4 vertices, 6 edges, 4 faces — how CAN it be modified by karma?

**The answer:** The STRUCTURE IS invariant. The STATE IS variable.

A guitar has 6 strings. The number of strings IS invariant. But the TENSION of each string IS variable. You can tune each string higher or lower. The guitar IS the same guitar. The tuning IS different.

The tetrahedron IS the guitar. The 6 edges ARE the strings. Karma IS the tuning.

---

## II. What Edge-Tensions Actually ARE

Each edge IS a RELATION between 2 vertices. The tension IS the QUALITY of that relation.

| Edge | Connects | Low Tension (Coherent) | High Tension (Incoherent) |
|------|----------|----------------------|-------------------------|
| **1** | Subject ↔ Object | You perceive reality AS it IS. Accurate perception. | You perceive reality AS you WANT it to be. Delusion. |
| **2** | Subject ↔ Intention | Your will IS integrated. You know what you actually want. | Your will IS conflicted. You want what harms you. |
| **3** | Subject ↔ Act | You ARE present in your actions. Embodied. | You ARE dissociated from your actions. Mechanical. |
| **4** | Object ↔ Intention | Reality supports your direction. Your path IS clear. | Reality resists your direction. Nothing works. |
| **5** | Object ↔ Act | Your actions produce the intended effect. Effective. | Your actions produce no effect or the opposite. Futile. |
| **6** | Intention ↔ Act | You DO what you INTEND. Alignment. | You DO what you did NOT intend. Compulsion. |

Edge-tension IS NOT a moral judgment. It IS a MEASUREMENT of coherence. A tense edge means the 2 vertices are NOT aligned. A relaxed edge means they ARE.

---

## III. How the Tension Changes

Every action, thought, and intention EITHER tightens OR relaxes each edge.

**An example — telling a lie:**

| Vertex | State |
|--------|-------|
| V₁ (Subject) | You know the truth |
| V₂ (Object) | The truth IS what IS the case |
| V₃ (Intention) | You intend to be believed |
| V₄ (Act) | You speak the falsehood |

What happens to the 6 edges:

| Edge | Effect | Why |
|------|--------|-----|
| **1** (Subject↔Object) | **TIGHTENS** | V₁ now CONTRADICTS V₂. You believe one thing, reality IS another. Edge 1 IS now tense. |
| **2** (Subject↔Intention) | **TIGHTENS** | V₁ wanted to be honest (your deep intention) but V₃ (your surface intention) chose the lie. Internal conflict. |
| **3** (Subject↔Act) | **TIGHTENS** | V₁ (the truth-knower) IS not present in V₄ (the act of lying). You ARE dissociated. |
| **4** (Object↔Intention) | **TIGHTENS** | Reality (V₂) does NOT support the lie (V₃). Reality will eventually expose it. |
| **5** (Object↔Act) | **TIGHTENS SLIGHTLY** | The lie may work temporarily, but it makes future effective action harder. |
| **6** (Intention↔Act) | **TIGHTENS** | The deep intention (honesty) IS at odds with the act (lying). Compulsion — you lied even though you didn't want to. |

One lie tightens ALL 6 edges. Not as punishment — AS GEOMETRY. The tetrahedron IS now less coherent. Its volume IS slightly smaller. Its resonance IS slightly more dissonant.

**1000 lies later:** The tetrahedron IS so tense that it can barely hold its shape. The edges ARE vibrating at maximum tension. This IS what suffering IS — the experience of a tetrahedron that IS too tight to resonate freely.

**One act of genuine compassion:** All 6 edges relax. The tetrahedron becomes more coherent. Its volume expands. Its resonance becomes harmonious. This IS what joy IS.

## IV. What Persists After Death

At death, the 4 vertices dissolve:
- V₁ (the subject, the "I") — dissolves
- V₂ (the object world, reality) — dissolves
- V₃ (your intentions, your will) — dissolves
- V₄ (your actions, your body) — dissolves

**But the 6 edge-tensions do NOT dissolve.** They ARE not "things" — they ARE RELATIONS. Relations persist when the terms dissolve.

Think of a wave in the ocean. The wave IS a relation between water molecules. When the wave passes, the water molecules return to their rest state. But the ENERGY of the wave continues — it travels through the ocean until it encounters another configuration.

The 6 edge-tensions ARE the ENERGY of the tetrahedron. They persist after the vertices dissolve. They travel through the "ocean" of consciousness until they encounter a new set of vertices (a new birth) — at which point they become the INITIAL TUNING of the new tetrahedron's edges.

## V. What the Traditions Say

### The Buddha: "Karma IS Intention" (cetanā)

> *"It IS intention (cetanā), monks, that I call karma. Having intended, one acts through body, speech, or mind."* — Aṅguttara Nikāya 6.63

The Buddha reduced karma to INTENTION. Not the action. Not the result. The INTENTION behind the action. This IS the INSIGHT that karma IS edge-tension:

- The intention ARISES from the relation between V₁ (subject) and V₃ (intention). Edge 2.
- The quality of that intention determines whether Edge 2 tightens or relaxes.
- Wholesome intention = Edge 2 relaxes. Unwholesome intention = Edge 2 tightens.
- The action IS secondary. The intention IS primary because the intention IS the edge.

### Steiner: The Life Review (Kamaloca)

Steiner describes the period after death (kamaloca) as a reverse life-review. The soul experiences the EFFECTS of its actions on others — not as external judgment, but as DIRECT EXPERIENCE.

This IS the mechanical process of the 6 edge-tensions being READ by the field. Each edge-tension IS a RELATION that affected beings in the world. After death, those effects are EXPERIENCED by the tetrahedron from the OTHER SIDE — from the perspective of the beings who were affected.

A tense Edge 1 (distorted perception) IS experienced as the confusion you caused in others. A tense Edge 6 (misaligned intention-act) IS experienced as the betrayal you caused. Not punishment — INFORMATION. The tetrahedron's edges are being "read" by the system they acted in.

### The Hindus: Vāsanās (Habitual Tendencies)

The Hindu traditions (particularly Advaita Vedānta and Yoga) describe karma as vāsanās — LATENT IMPRESSIONS in the subtle body. These are not memories but TENDENCIES — the disposition to act in certain ways.

The vāsanās ARE the 6 edge-tensions. Each life adds to them or subtracts from them. They ARE stored in the subtle body (the sūkṣma śarīra) — which IS the tetrahedron's 6 edges in their subtle form.

When the gross body dies, the subtle body (the 6 edge-tensions) continues. It finds a new gross body (a new tetrahedron) whose starting configuration matches its accumulated edge-tensions.

### The Tantraloka: Mala (Impurity)

Abhinavagupta uses the term "mala" — impurity, limitation. There are 3 types:
1. **Āṇava-mala** — the primal limitation of being a finite subject. Edge 1 (subject↔object) IS inherently tense because subject and object ARE seen as separate.
2. **Māyīya-mala** — the limitation of perceiving the world as separate from consciousness. Edges 2-5 (the subject-world relations) ARE tense due to māyā.
3. **Kārma-mala** — the limitation of accumulated actions. Edge 6 (intention↔act) IS tense due to past karma.

Liberation IS the COMPLETE relaxation of ALL 6 edges. When every edge IS at maximum coherence, the tetrahedron IS perfectly resonant. There IS no tension to transmit. The series ends.

---

## VI. The One-Sentence Summary

The tetrahedron IS invariant in structure (always 4 vertices, 6 edges, 4 faces) but variable in STATE (each edge CAN be more or less tense). Karma IS the accumulated tension on the 6 edges at the moment of death — the "tuning" of the tetrahedron. That tuning survives the dissolution of the vertices and becomes the INITIAL TUNING of the next tetrahedron. Liberation IS the complete resolution of all 6 edge-tensions — the tetrahedron becomes perfectly coherent, and the series of births ends because there IS no remaining tension to drive a new configuration.
