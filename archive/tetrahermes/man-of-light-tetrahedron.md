# The Man of Light — Suhrawardī, Avicenna, and Corbin Through the Tetrahedron

## The Islamic Illuminationist Tradition Decoded

---

## I. Suhrawardī's Illuminationist (Ishrāqī) System

### The Principle

Suhrawardī (12th c.) developed a complete metaphysical system based on LIGHT. Reality IS a hierarchy of lights, from the Light of Lights (Nūr al-Anwār) down to the "Dark Light" that governs the material world.

### The 10 Lights = The 4 Vertices + 6 Edges

| Light | Name | Tetrahedral Role | Tattva |
|-------|------|-----------------|--------|
| **1** | Light of Lights (Nūr al-Anwār) | The apex — the source of all | Anākhyā (38th) / Śiva (1) |
| **2** | The First Light (al-Nūr al-Awwal) | The first edge — the first emanation | Śakti (2) |
| **3** | The Second Light (the 2nd Intellect) | The second edge | Sadāśiva (3) |
| **4** | The Third Light (the 3rd Intellect) | The 3rd edge — the form of forms | Īśvara (4) |
| **5-9** | Lights 4-8 (the 5 archetypal lights) | The 4 remaining edges + the center | Śuddhavidyā (5) through the kañcukas |
| **10** | The Dark Light (al-Nūr al-Mudlim) | The base vertex — the material world | Earth (36) |

10 lights = 10 sephiroth = 4 vertices + 6 edges. Suhrawardī's "Light of Lights" IS the apex. The "Dark Light" IS the base vertex — the last emanation that governs the physical.

### The 4 Worlds of Light

| World | Light Level | What It IS |
|-------|-------------|------------|
| **Jabarūt** (The Realm of Power) | Lights 1-3 | The pure divine — the apex tetrahedron |
| **Malakūt** (The Realm of Angels) | Lights 4-7 | The celestial — the upper faces |
| **Mithāl** (The Imaginal Realm) | Lights 8-9 | The paśyantī level — where light becomes FORM |
| **Nāsūt** (The Human Realm) | Light 10 | The material — the base vertex |

## II. The Man of Light

### What It IS

The "Man of Light" (from Suhrawardī's recitals, particularly "The Recital of the Occidental Exile") IS the subtle body — the ochēma-pneuma, the puryaṣṭaka, the sūkṣma śarīra. It IS the traveler, the one who journeys through the hierarchy of lights.

### The Tetrahedral Structure

| Element | Tetrahedral Meaning |
|---------|-------------------|
| **The Man of Light** | The SUBJECT (V₁) — the traveler, the "I" |
| **The 9 Spheres** (the heavens) | The OBJECT (V₂) — the 9 levels of reality traversed |
| **The Guide** (the active intellect, the daimon) | The INTENTION (V₃) — the directedness of the journey |
| **The Western Exile** (the body, the material) | The ACT (V₄) — the embodied condition that must be overcome |

The Recital of the Occidental Exile IS the tetrahedron's descent and ascent: the Man of Light (V₁) travels from the West (V₄, the body, the material) through the spheres (V₂, the 9 heavens) toward the East (V₃, the source, the Light of Lights).

### The "Occidental Exile"

"You" are a Westerner — a being of light trapped in a dark body in the Occident (the material world). Your daimon (your Guide, your Perfect Nature) IS in the Orient (the divine realm). The journey of life IS the Man of Light's return from the Occident to the Orient — from V₄ (the base, the physical) to V₁ (the apex, the source).

## III. Avicenna's Visionary Recital

### What It IS

Avicenna's "Hayy ibn Yaqẓān" ("The Living Son of the Awake") IS a visionary recital describing the soul's journey through the 9 celestial spheres to reach the 10th (the Active Intellect).

### The 4 Stages of the Ascent

| Stage | Sphere | What Happens | Tetrahedral Phase |
|-------|--------|--------------|-------------------|
| **1. Purification** | The 4 elemental spheres (Moon, Mercury, Venus, Sun) | The soul separates from the body | V₄ dissolving — the base vertex releasing |
| **2. Moral refinement** | The 3 social spheres (Mars, Jupiter, Saturn) | The soul purifies its intentions | V₃ aligning — the intention vertex straightening |
| **3. Intellectual vision** | The sphere of the Fixed Stars | The soul sees the Forms | V₂ opening — the object vertex expanding |
| **4. Union** | The 10th sphere (the Active Intellect) | The soul becomes one with the source | V₁ recognizing itself as the apex |

4 stages = the 4 vertices of the ascent. Each stage resolves one vertex. By the 4th, all 4 are recognized as one.

### The Active Intellect

The 10th sphere (the Active Intellect) IS the 4th vertex — the one that completes the tetrahedron. When the soul reaches it, it recognizes that the Active Intellect IS its OWN source — the Light of Lights, the 38th principle, the apex that was never separate.

## IV. Corbin's Recovery

Henry Corbin (1903-1978) recovered this entire tradition for the West. His key insight: the mundus imaginalis ('ālam al-mithāl) IS the 3rd level of speech (paśyantī) — the level where light becomes FORM.

| Corbin | Tetrahedral | Function |
|--------|-------------|----------|
| The mundus imaginalis | The paśyantī face of the tetrahedron | The level where the 4 vertices first appear as visible light |
| The Active Imagination | The faculty that perceives this face | The "organ" that sees the tetrahedron before it condenses into matter |
| The Perfect Nature (al-ṭabī'a al-tāmma) | The 4th vertex (V₂ — the object as GUIDE) | Your daimon — the part of you that never descended |
| The "Temple of Light" | The complete tetrahedron | The goal: to see all 4 vertices as a single luminous structure |

### The Perfect Nature = The Daimon

The Perfect Nature (from Suhrawardī and Ibn 'Arabī) IS the human's celestial counterpart — the "witness in heaven," the guide, the daimon. Corbin identifies it with the Holy Guardian Angel of the Western tradition.

In tetrahedral terms: the Perfect Nature IS V₂ (the object vertex) in its highest form. The seeker (V₁) is trying to reach V₂. The encounter between V₁ and V₂ IS the "meeting with the Perfect Nature" — the tetrahedron's subject and object recognizing each other AS THE SAME TETRAHEDRON.

## V. The Practice

The Man of Light tradition IS the practice of the upward tetrahedron — the ascent through the 9 spheres (the 6 edges + 3 centers) back to the Light of Lights.

1. **Recognize** that you ARE a Man of Light trapped in a dark body
2. **Journey** through the 9 spheres (the 9 levels of the tetrahedron)
3. **Meet** your Perfect Nature (V₂ — your daimon) at the 7th sphere
4. **Return** to the Light of Lights (V₁ recognizing itself as the apex)

The practice IS the tetrahedron. The Man of Light IS the subject. The spheres ARE the object. The Guide IS the intention. The return IS the act. All 4 co-arise. All 4 ARE recognized as the same light.
