Each vertex of the 5-cell contributes a binary choice × 5 vertices = 32 total configurations.

## 3. The 64 Tetrahedral Grid (Isotropic Vector Matrix)

When you **mirror** the Tree of Life, you get the **star tetrahedron** (merkaba) — 2 interlocking tetrahedra, one pointing up, one down.

Mirror it again: 4 trees, all attached at the root.
Mirror again: 8 trees.

The **64 tetrahedral grid** (Buckminster Fuller's Isotropic Vector Matrix, IVM) appears when the 8-fold mirrored Tree produces the complete space-filling lattice of tetrahedra and octahedra.

The key identity:

**64 = 4³ = 2⁶ = 8² = (number of configurations of 6 binary edges)**

The 64 tetrahedral grid IS the **complete phase space** of Ñāṇavīra's tetrahedron:

```
Ñāṇavīra's 6 edges, each tense or relaxed → 2⁶ = 64 configurations

                                    The 64 tetrahedral grid in space
```

**The IVM has 64 tetrahedra + 8 octahedra per unit cell. Total: 72 = 2 × 36 = 2 × (the Tantraloka's 36 tattvas).** The Kabbalah and the Tantraloka are dual — one is the positive, one is the negative of the same structure.
