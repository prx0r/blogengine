# Beat Map — Script 04: The 36 Tattvas
## Scene Type: manim-geometry (THREE.js wireframe for tetrahedra)
## Duration: ~4:30

---

### B1 [0:00-0:15] "There is a map. It has 36 levels."
**VISUAL:** Pure black void (#0D1117). A single fine gold (#D4A574) horizontal line appears center-screen, stretching edge to edge — a baseline. Above it, 36 small gold dots appear in sequence, left to right, like markings on a ruler. At the leftmost dot, a tiny white (#E6E1DC) label fades in: "Śiva." At the rightmost: "Earth."

**AUDIO:** Single deep fundamental tone at 130Hz (C3), held with slow attack. No notes yet — just the drone of pure potential.

---

### B2 [0:15-0:30] "Consciousness, thickening."
**VISUAL:** The baseline begins to tip — left side rises, right side sinks. The 36 dots now form a descending staircase. Each dot pulses gold briefly as it's mentioned. White subtitle fades bottom-center: "36 Tattvas — Abhinavagupta, Kashmir, ~1000 CE."

**AUDIO:** Tone.js begins a descending arpeggio — 36 notes total, one per dot, each note 100ms, dropping from ~800Hz to ~80Hz. Volume -28dB. The 36th note (earth) is the deepest, almost sub-bass rumble.

---

### B3 [0:30-1:00] "A tattva is a principle — a level of reality..."
**VISUAL:** Each of the 36 dots transforms into a small tetrahedron wireframe (gold edges, no fill). Nine groups of four tetrahedra form — arranged in a 3×3 nesting pattern. Camera zooms slowly into the central tetrahedron. Purple (#A855F7) wireframe highlights a single edge — one tattva. White label: "tat = that" → fades. "A tattva is a THAT-ness."

**AUDIO:** Ambient: sparse harmonic intervals (perfect fifths, octaves) at 8% probability/frame. Each tetrahedron appearance triggers a single soft chime — gold notes at 523Hz (C5) with 3s release.

---

### B4 [1:00-1:30] "The descent is not historical. It happens now."
**VISUAL:** The tetrahedron structure pulses — one full expansion-contraction cycle, like a heartbeat. Gold edges brighten → dim → brighten. A single white dot appears at the center of the innermost tetrahedron — labeled "YOU." The whole structure rotates slowly (one full rotation over 30s) to reveal all edges. Text subtitle: "The 36 tattvas are the anatomy of a single instant."

**AUDIO:** The descending arpeggio reverses — now ascending, low to high, same 36 notes but rising. The cycle of descent and return. A subtle heartbeat-like low-frequency pulse (40Hz, felt not heard) under the notes.

---

### B5 [1:30-2:00] "At the top, the pure tattvas — Śiva, Śakti..."
**VISUAL:** The top portion of the structure illuminates — five tetrahedra glow gold at their vertices. Labels appear at each vertex in white serif text: Śiva, Śakti, Sadāśiva, Īśvara, Śuddhavidyā. These five vertices are brighter than anything else — luminosity without source. The camera slowly orbits around this upper cluster.

**AUDIO:** Five-note chord in just intonation (harmonic series: 1:2:3:4:5 ratios) — pure, consonant, no beating. Held for the full 30s with gentle fade. Volume -30dB.

---

### B6 [2:00-2:20] "Then the coverings — the kañcukas."
**VISUAL:** Five translucent purple (#A855F7, opacity 0.4) shells appear, each wrapping around the inner tetrahedra like nested spheres. Labels fade in one by one on each shell: "Time," "Space," "Desire," "Knowledge," "Agency." Each shell contracts slightly as it appears — constricting, like a fist closing.

**AUDIO:** The pure chord begins to detune slightly — subtle beating at 2-3Hz. Each kañcuka appearance adds a slight dissonance. The sound of contraction.

---

### B7 [2:20-2:30] "Then the mind — the internal instruments."
**VISUAL:** Three turquoise (#14B8A6, opacity 0.5) triangular planes appear mid-structure — Intellect (top), Ego (center), Manas (bottom). They act as filters, each plane slightly warping the light that passes through from above. Ray-like gold lines hit each plane and scatter.

**AUDIO:** The tone shifts to a mid-range hum (330Hz, E4) — the frequency of cognition. Slightly filtered, slightly veiled. 8% chance/frame of soft harmonics.

---

### B8 [2:30-3:30] "Here's the geometry. The 36 tattvas are not a list."
**VISUAL:** Full structural reveal. Camera pulls back. The entire nine-tetrahedron nest is visible — each tetrahedron wireframe rendered in gold, some edges purple (the 36 tattvas are the edges). An animated overlay: 9 tetrahedra × 6 edges = 54 → minus 18 shared edges = 36 edges. The shared edges pulse purple, then gold. White equation text appears top-right: "9 × 6 − 18 = 36." Each of the 36 edges illuminates sequentially with a gold pulse. Zoom into a single edge — label appears: "This edge IS water." The edge transforms briefly into a flowing particle stream.

**AUDIO:** The full 36-note scale plays — each note corresponds to one edge illuminating. After all 36 notes play, a resonant chord holds all 36 frequencies simultaneously for a moment — then resolves to silence. The edge labeled "water" gets a flowing, liquid-like arpeggio (four notes cycling).

---

### B9 [3:30-4:00] "The direction is upward."
**VISUAL:** A gold arrow appears at the bottom of the structure, pointing upward. All 36 edges glow in sequence from bottom to top in a wave — like a scan line moving up. The arrow pulses. White subtitle: "Contraction → Expansion." Then: "Tattva 36 → Tattva 1." The camera slowly ascends through the structure.

**AUDIO:** Ascending arpeggio — rising through all 36 notes, slow and deliberate, each note at the corresponding edge's visual pulse. Tone.js frequency sweep from 80Hz to 800Hz over 30s.

---

### B10 [4:00-4:30] "You are consciousness undergoing the entire 36-level contraction right now."
**VISUAL:** Return to the single white dot from B4 — "YOU" — at the center of the structure. The tetrahedra fade to 20% opacity. The dot pulses gold, then a subtle green (#166534) glow appears around it — the heart center. The structure fades out except for the dot, which becomes a single point of gold light in void. Gold text fades center: "The pull upward is already there." Hold. Fade to black.

**AUDIO:** All 36 notes merge into a single fundamental at 130Hz (C3) — the same drone from B1. A single high harmonic (1046Hz, C6) floats above it. Then silence. The drone fades to nothing over the final 3 seconds.
