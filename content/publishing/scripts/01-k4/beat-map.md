  AUDIO: Low C2 drone (65Hz) at -30dB, barely felt. No beats yet.

BEAT 2 [0:20-0:35] "...has exactly four parts."
  VISUAL: Three more gold points materialize at tetrahedron vertices around "YOU". Dim dashed edges (#8B7355) preview connections. Each point pulses gently.
  TEXT: "SUBJECT" at top vertex, "OBJECT" bottom-left, "INTENTION" top-right, "ACT" bottom-right. White, Inter Light, small.
  AUDIO: G2 (98Hz) joins drone. Perfect fifth interval.

BEAT 3 [0:35-1:05] "Here they are. Try it now..."
  VISUAL: First-person simulation: one gold point center. Three ghost points orbit. When narration says "these words you're hearing" — one ghost brightens to show object. "Your attention directed" — intention edge pulses. "The actual happening" — act edge completes, tetrahedron solidifies momentarily gold. Then resets. Loops as the explanation continues.
  AUDIO: C3 (131Hz) filter sweep — slight brightening as tetrahedron completes.

BEAT 4 [1:05-1:45] "Why four? Why not three?"
  VISUAL: Triangle (3 points, 3 edges) appears — flat, grey (#6B7280), no fill. Text "FLAT" appears. Then 4th point rises from center of triangle — slow emergence, purple glow. Six gold edges snap into place. Tetrahedron now has volume — subtle inner glow (dark green, #166534, opacity 0.15 in center). Text "VOLUME" replaces "FLAT".
  AUDIO: Major chord (C-E-G) at tetrahedron completion. Before that: single note, tense.

BEAT 5 [1:45-2:45] "Four independent discoveries..."
  VISUAL: Timeline appears across screen. Four tetrahedra appear at their dates:
    - 475 CE: Proclus tetrahedron (gold, Greek label "HENAD·BEING·LIFE·INTELLECT")  
    - 1000 CE: Abhinavagupta tetrahedron (gold, Sanskrit label "UPĀYA")
    - 1963: Ñāṇavīra tetrahedron (gold, "SUBJECT·OBJECT·INTENTION·ACT")
    - 2013: Amplituhedron (gold, "A₄₂ · 4 MOMENTA · 6 BCFW")
  All four tetrahedra rotate slowly in sync. Text "1,500 years · 4 cultures · 1 shape" fades in.
  AUDIO: Harmonic series — C2, C3, G3, C4 — each joining as a new tetrahedron appears. Four-note chord sustained.

BEAT 6 [2:45-3:30] "Ñāṇavīra called this the Fundamental Structure."
  VISUAL: Return to single tetrahedron. Zoom into center — the green core (#00FF88) becomes visible. Then zoom out: multiple nested tetrahedra at different scales — a cell (Markov blanket), a brain, a body, a galaxy. Same 4-vertex/6-edge structure at every scale. Text label on each.
  AUDIO: Ambient — soft choral pad, no attack. The green core moment: subtle high shimmer (8kHz+).

BEAT 7 [3:30-4:15] "You are one of its vertices."
  VISUAL: Return to first-person view from Beat 3. Gold point = YOU. Try to move away — the tetrahedron reforms. Try to look at yourself — old YOU becomes OBJECT, new YOU appears. Can't escape. Text: "The attempt to leave IS the tetrahedron."
  AUDIO: Drone drops out. Silence for 2 seconds. Then single low C2 held.

BEAT 8 [4:15-4:30] "There is no outside."
  VISUAL: Tetrahedron rotates slowly. All four vertices glow equal gold. Green core brightens. Text fades: "The minimum. The invariant. The shape of having a world at all." Fade to black.
  AUDIO: Final chord — C major spread across 3 octaves, slow decay over 10 seconds. Silence at black.
