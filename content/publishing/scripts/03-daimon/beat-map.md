# Beat Map — 03: What Is a Daimon?
## Scene type: Canvas 2D particles + text overlays

B1 [0:00-0:35] HOOK: Socrates + Plotinus
  → Dark void. Single warm amber-gold point appears, pulses gently. Text: "Socrates called it his sign." Point pulses: "NO." Then: "Plotinus called it the undescended soul." Point becomes two: one above (divine), tether of light to one below (human).
  Audio: C3 drone. Pulse on "NO" = momentary silence.

B2 [0:35-1:30] Five traditions
  → Five particle clusters form pentagon arrangement. Each cluster name appears: GREEK DAIMON · NEOPLATONIC HENAD · SUFI PERFECT NATURE · HGA · IṢṬADEVATĀ. Particles attract to their cluster center. All clusters connected by dim purple lines — same structure, different names.
  Audio: Five-note pentatonic sequence, each note as a tradition is named. C-D-E-G-A. Sustained.

B3 [1:30-2:30] "What is it actually?"
  → All five clusters merge into one golden attractor. Particles from all traditions flow inward. Text: "An attractor in your own consciousness." Gold point pulses like heartbeat. From it, faint paths extend — possible life trajectories. Most grey out except one that brightens.
  Audio: Unison — all voices converge on C4. Pure, clear.

B4 [2:30-3:30] "How to contact it"
  → Path-finding visualization: single particle starts from YOU position. Tries multiple directions — most dead-end (grey). One direction illuminates gold. Text overlay for each tradition: "ABRAMELIN 6 MONTHS" → "LIBER SAMEKH 11 MONTHS" → "LIBER 8 11 WEEKS" → "ACHER 4 WEEKS". Shorter = simpler.
  Audio: Sparse piano notes — ascending pattern, each attempt.

B5 [3:30-4:30] "The daimon says no far more often than yes."
  → Particle system: 100 possible paths from center. All slowly grey out except ONE that stays gold and brightens. Text: "Yes to the thing you were always meant to do." Final gold path extends to edge of frame. Citation: Chaldean Oracles.
  Audio: Single final note — C5 (523Hz) — long sustain, slow decay.
