# Universal Visual Vocabulary

A reusable library of abstract visual metaphors. Each visual teaches ONE conceptual operation. They can be sequenced, layered, and remixed across any tradition.

---

## The Core Alphabet (15 Visual Operations)

These 15 visual metaphors cover 90% of abstract concepts across all traditions. Each is a 10-15s LTX clip.

| # | Visual | What It Teaches | Works For |
|---|--------|-----------------|-----------|
| **V1** | **Emanation** — Light flowing downward from a source, cascading through layers | How the One becomes many without diminishing itself | Tattvas, Neoplatonic emanation, Sufi creation, 99 Names, celestial hierarchy |
| **V2** | **Contraction** — Concentric rings closing inward, space darkening | How infinity becomes limitation | Māyā, Kañcukas, soul entering matter, ego formation, the Fall |
| **V3** | **Expansion** — Concentric rings radiating outward, space brightening | How limitation dissolves back into infinity | Recognition, liberation, enlightenment, fanā, nirvāṇa |
| **V4** | **Pulse/Vibration** — Rhythmic brightening/dimming at a center point, waves emanating | The substrate of all experience | Spanda, heartbeat of existence, ōm, divine breath, the Word |
| **V5** | **Descent** — Something falling/flowing downward from above | Grace, revelation, inspiration | Śaktipāta, theophany, angel descending, wahy (revelation), barakah |
| **V6** | **Ascent** — Something rising upward from below | Spiritual progress, awakening | Kuṇḍalinī, mi'rāj (ascension), ladder of being, prayer rising |
| **V7** | **Reflection/Mirror** — Two figures/forms mirroring each other across a vertical axis | Correspondence, recognition | Pratyabhijñā, as above so below, theophany, imago Dei, perfect man |
| **V8** | **Dissolution** — A solid form breaking apart into particles | Emptiness, impermanence, non-self | Śūnyatā, anitya, fanā, death of ego, deconstruction |
| **V9** | **Bloom** — A single point expanding into a complex colored form | Manifestation, beauty, emotion transformed | Rasa, creation, theophany, imagination, beauty |
| **V10** | **Veil** — A semi-transparent layer passing over a form | Hiddenness, revelation, the gap between | Hijāb, Māyā as veil, parda, apophatic theology, the concealed |
| **V11** | **Thread/Connection** — A luminous line connecting two points | Relationship, causality, transmission | Silsila (lineage), guru-śiṣya, daimon leading, chain of being |
| **V12** | **Ladder/Stair** — A vertical structure with distinct levels | Hierarchy, stages of progress | Seven chakras, four worlds, three upāyas, stages of the path |
| **V13** | **Center/Periphery** — A radial pattern with everything pointing to/from center | Unity, integrated vs fragmented | Tawḥīd, maṇḍala, sūtra (thread through all), return to center |
| **V14** | **Threshold/Arch** — A doorway or boundary being crossed | Transition, initiation, liminal space | Dīkṣā (initiation), barzakh, birth/death, conversion, the Gate |
| **V15** | **Breath** — A form expanding and contracting in a steady wave | Life, spirit, connection | Prāṇa, rūḥ (spirit), pneuma, the breath of the compassionate |

## How to Use

1. **Pick a concept** from the BIG list (e.g., #22 Ascension)
2. **Look up its core visuals** (V6 Ascent + V12 Ladder)
3. **Pick the tradition** → find the mapping in the table
4. **Craft the LTX prompt** using the visual description
5. **Store the clip** tagged by visual ID (#V6), not by concept name — so it's reusable

**Example:**
```
Concept: #22 Ascension (Sufi version)
Visuals: V6 + V12
Clip stored as: V6_ASCENSION_SUFI.mp4
Also usable for: Krishna's ascent in Gita, Buddha's parinirvāṇa,
                   Hermetic ascent through spheres
Tags: [V6] [V12] [ascension] [liberation] [sufi] [neoplatonic]
```

## Relationship to the Production Queue

The 80 concepts above map onto the 44 clips in the production queue. Each clip in the queue generates one of these visuals. That clip is then reusable across any video that needs that concept, in any tradition.

The visual vocabulary is the *permanent library*. Specific explainer prompts live in `concept-explainers.md`. The production queue in `gpu-production-queue.md` is the *immediate build list*. This document is the *design system* that links them.
