# doorway prompts — the meditation anchor

one sequence. seven locations. no identifiable gender. simple hands, simple clothes, breath.

**the beat:** already seated → open eyes → look left, look right → breathe in → breathe out → close eyes → black

---

## 1. FOREST CLEARING — morning, birds, green

POV first-person. You are already seated cross-legged on a mossy rock in a forest clearing. Your hands rest on your knees — simple hands, no rings, no polish, relaxed fingers. Dark neutral pants. The forest around you is green and gold. Morning light slants through the canopy, illuminating patches of moss and fern. The air is slightly cool.

Your eyes open. You look straight ahead at the trees, the light, the distant mist. Then slowly turn your head to the left — ferns, a fallen log, a patch of sunlight on moss. Then slowly turn your head to the right — a stream, stones, leaves moving in the breeze. You take a slow deep breath — your chest rises, the air fills. You exhale, your chest falls. Your eyes close. The forest light softens, the green fades to warm grey, then black.

Fades to black in 3 seconds after eyes close.

Sound: birds, stream, wind in leaves, one deep breath, exhale.

## THE RETURN — open eyes at the end

Black screen. One slow breath — the inhale begins. As you exhale, your eyes open. Your hands come back into focus — same hands, same knees, same posture. The world reassembles — the light, the sound, the place you began. You look down at your hands for a moment. The meditation is complete.

Duration: 3 seconds from black to full return. Sound: the same ambient audio as the opening, returning gently.
