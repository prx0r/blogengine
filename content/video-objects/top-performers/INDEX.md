## Quick Reference: Title Patterns That Work

| Pattern | Formula | Best For | Example |
|---|---|---|---|
| **A** (Revelation) | "X is NOT what you think" | Undermining common beliefs | SHIVA is NOT what you think |
| **B** (Esoteric Reveal) | "The Forgotten/Secret X" | Undiscovered knowledge | The Forgotten Realm Inside You |
| **C** (Define) | "What Is X?" | Jargon people can't define | What Is Theurgy? |
| **D** (Mechanism) | "Why X Works" | Causal explanation | Why Ritual Magick Works |
| **E** (Bridge) | "X is Y's Z" | Classic + contemporary | The Odyssey is Christopher Nolan's Trick |
| **F** (Access) | "X Explained" | Safe but low breakout | The ABCs of Platonism |
