## Analysis

### Title Mechanism
Uses Pattern A (X is NOT what you think) — the classic revelation framework. "SHIVA" is capitalized for maximum search and thumbnail visibility. The subtitle "Upanishads Reveals the Truth" appends scriptural authority to the contrarian hook. The trailing ellipsis creates a completion impulse. This is the most aggressive title in the dataset and also the shortest video (5:33), proving that a provocative hook + authoritative framing + concise delivery is a winning formula for philosophical content.

### Why It Worked
Massive demand for Hindu philosophical content in English is being poorly served. A 2.2k-sub channel pulling 260k views is definitive proof. The video promises to correct a common misconception using primary scripture (Upanishads), which positions the viewer as someone who wants "the real truth" rather than popular mythology. At 5:33, the video respects the viewer's time while delivering a tight, focused argument. The title is designed to generate strong reactions — people either click because they're curious or because they disagree and want to comment. Both drive the algorithm. The "reveals the truth" framing implies insider knowledge, which is a powerful psychological trigger.

### What to Borrow
