# Best Seed Image Picks

## Overview

For each world, 10-15 images were selected from museum search results based on:
- Relevance to the world theme
- Valid image URL (commons/cleveland/AIC public domain or CC0)
- High quality and actual artwork (not PDFs, book covers, or lunar craters)

Images are downloaded to `{world}/originals/`.
