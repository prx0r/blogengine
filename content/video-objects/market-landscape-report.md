## Tier 3: Saturated or Lower Signal

### 7. Alchemy Philosophy
**Breakouts dominated by:** Paulo Coelho audiobooks (1.5M views), Emerald Tablets audio (317k). Self-help "alchemy" is saturated. Historical philosophical alchemy (Khunrath, Ripley, Sendivogius) is emptier but also lower search volume. Best approached as specific historical deep-dives rather than "what is alchemy."

**Approach:** Make specific films (e.g., "The Ripley Scroll Explained") rather than broad alchemy content.

### 8. Buddhist Philosophy
Nagarjuna gets strong views but nearly all in Hindi/Bengali. English emptiness is real but the audience skews academic. Best approached as comparative (Nagarjuna + Nanavira + modern consciousness).

### 9. Consciousness & Physics
Highest avg views/day after Literature (8,736) but dominated by big channels (Brian Cox, Arvin Ash). Hard to break in as a small channel. Best angle: the specific heterodox theories nobody covers (Friston, Barandes, ORCH-OR, QRI).
