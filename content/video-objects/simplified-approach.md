# Simplified Approach — What LTX Actually Generates

## The AfterSkool Insight

AfterSkool's formula: **~24 reusable visual types + one concept per video + 5-8 min.** They don't create new visuals for each video. They remix the same visual vocabulary.

We do the same. **LTX generates ~24 reusable clip types.** Every video remixes them.

## The 20-Minute Video Template (Revised)

With 26 reusable clip types, a 20-min video looks like:

```
[0:00-1:00]  HOOK — Quote on atmospheric background (Quote card)
[1:00-2:30]  INTRO — A1 Shadowed Guru + narration setting the context
[2:30-3:30]  THE QUESTION — A2 Seeker Walking + narrator asks the question
[3:30-5:00]  CONTRAST — B2 Contraction (problem) or B1 Emanation (context)
[5:00-7:00]  THE TEACHING — Still art from tradition (zero LTX cost)
[7:00-7:30]  VISUALIZE — B4 Pulse or B9 Bloom (LTX abstract concept)
[7:30-10:00] DEEPEN — Still art continues
[10:00-11:00] QUOTE — Text on A4 Mountains/Dawn background
[11:00-13:00] STORY — C3 Guru-Disciple or C2 Vision (LTX story arc scene)
[13:00-15:00] APPLICATION — Still art
[15:00-15:30] THE SHIFT — B3 Expansion (LTX — the recognition/liberation moment)
[15:30-17:00] SYNTHESIS — Still art
[17:00-18:00] CLOSE — A1 Shadowed Guru returns + closing quote
[18:00-20:00] OUTRO — A10 Path/Fork or A3 Consciousness as Light
```

**LTX clips per video: 8-10** (from the bank of 26)
**Still art segments: 6-8** (from museum images / Hareesh blog illustrations)
**Quote cards: 2-3**

These 5 LTX clips + narration about the specific yogi = any yogi story video.

## The Mantra

**26 clips is the entire visual universe.** Every video is a remix.

Don't think "what clip do I need for this video." Think "which of the 26 clips fits this segment."

When the GPU pod is ready, generate these 26 clips (1-2 variants each = ~60 generations). Then you have the complete visual library for every video the channel will ever make.
