# What Makes a Good LTX Prompt

## My Bad Prompt (what I did):
```
A single point of pulsating blue-white light at center in deep darkness. Concentric rings emanate outward. Static camera.
```
→ Static description. No narrative. No transformation. Result: shit.

## Their Good Prompt (what you showed):
```
Young girl with blue hair walking slowly down a dark ominous hallway, her face gradually transforming from neutral to a sinister evil smile, lighting progressively dimming darker and darker as she walks, shadows growing longer and more menacing.
```
→ A SEQUENCE. Things change over time. Emotional arc. Result: works.

## The Difference

| My approach | Their approach |
|-------------|---------------|
| Describes a scene | Describes a change over time |
| Static | Dynamic |
| No character | Specific character (girl, blue hair) |
| No emotional arc | Neutral → sinister smile |
| Lighting: one setting | Lighting: dims progressively |
| Vague "warm parchment tones" | Specific "gothic Victorian" |

## Rules for Good Prompts (from what worked)

1. **Something must CHANGE over the 10 seconds.** Not just "a light pulses" but "the light grows brighter, then dims, shadows stretch across the walls, a figure emerges from the darkness."

2. **Specific character details.** "Young girl with blue hair" > "a figure." "Button eyes reflecting dim light" > "clay-textured figure."

3. **Progressive lighting.** "Light dims as she walks" > "static lighting." The change in atmosphere is the visual interest.

4. **Emotional arc.** "Neutral → sinister smile" creates tension. The viewer stays to see what happens.

5. **World atmosphere first.** Establish the WORLD (dark parallel world, gothic Victorian, eerie stop-motion) before the specific action. The world IS the visual identity.

## New Prompt Structure

```
[WORLD ATMOSPHERE] + [CHARACTER] + [ACTION SEQUENCE] + [LIGHTING CHANGE] + [EMOTIONAL ARC]
```

Example for our content:
```
Stop-motion Laika Studios aesthetic, dark warm void with volcanic amber light pulsing from deep below. A small geometric clay figure stands at the edge of a vast circular mandala drawn in golden lines on stone. The mandala begins to glow beneath their feet, rings of light expanding outward one by one. With each ring, the figure's posture shifts — shoulders relaxing, head lifting, chest opening. The light climbs from their feet upward, illuminating them from below. By the final ring, they are radiant. Static camera, 24fps. Silence.
```
