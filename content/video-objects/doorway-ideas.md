### The Formula

Each doorway follows the same beat structure:

```
[GROUNDING] — Universal action (sitting, looking, touching)
[SHIFT] — The world begins to change (blur, fade, distort, expand)
[THRESHOLD] — The transition itself (fade to black/white, color bloom, light flood)
[ARRIVAL] — Arrival in imaginal realm / specific world
```

### For Prompts

When prompting these:
- Start with the physical detail (hands, knees, steam, pages)
- Describe the change (blur, soften, expand, fade)
- End with the transition (fills frame, becomes, fades to)
- Keep it POV, keep it simple, no characters
