# North Star — The Ochema Format

## The Core Concept

The viewer IS the Seeker. First-person POV throughout.

## The Structure

**The Imaginal Realm (liminal white space)**
- The space between worlds. Cream-white void, infinite, soft.
- The viewer arrives here between each world transition.
- It is itself a teaching: neither here nor there, the space where imagination meets reality.

**The Doorway**
- Each world is entered through a doorway — an arch, a gate, a curtain of light.
- The viewer walks toward it, passes through, emerges in a different tradition.
- The Daimon may appear here to guide.

**The Daimon (north star)**
- A luminous figure made of soft warm light. No features.
- Appears in the white space between worlds.
- Gestures toward the next doorway. Walks ahead. Waits.

**The Worlds (organize videos by category)**
Each world is a self-contained aesthetic and philosophical space:

| World | Mood | Colors | For videos about |
|-------|------|--------|------------------|
| **Kashmir** | Golden, misty, ancient | Amber, gold, deep blue | Tantraloka, tattvas, spanda, rasa |
| **Sufi** | Moonlit, silver, mystical | Silver, indigo, green | Ibn Arabi, Suhrawardi, Corbin |
| **Neoplatonic** | Warm dusk, marble, torchlit | Marble white, midnight, gold | Proclus, Plotinus, the One |
| **Hermetic** | Candlelit, amber, dim | Deep red, aged parchment | Alchemy, Hermes, grimoires |
| **Buddhist** | Misty dawn, bamboo, green | Earth, sky, faded gold | Nagarjuna, sunyata, meditation |
| **Yoga** | Sunrise, mountain peaks | Saffron, white, sky blue | Yoga Sutras, prana, samadhi |

## The Video Structure

```
[WHITE SPACE] — We are in the imaginal realm. Soft void.
[DAIMON] — A luminous figure appears, gestures toward a doorway.
[POV WALK] — We walk toward the doorway. Warm light spills through.
[DOORWAY] — We pass through. Transition to WORLD.
[WORLD ESTABLISH] — We are sitting on a bench/stone in the world. Look around.
[TEACHING] — Voiceover unfolds as we sit, looking at our hands, the landscape.
[CUT TO] — Details: a book, a carving, a view. Always from our perspective.
[RETURN] — The Daimon appears again. We stand. Walk toward another doorway.
[WHITE SPACE] — Back in the imaginal. Ready for the next world.
```

## POV Shots That Work (queue these)

| Shot | What viewer sees | Use |
|------|-----------------|-----|
| POV_BENCH | Own hands on wooden bench, book beside | Establishing in a world |
| POV_DOORWAY | Walking toward stone arch with light beyond | Transition between worlds |
| POV_MEDITATE | Looking down at crossed legs on mountain | Contemplation segments |
| POV_DAIMON | Luminous figure approaching in white space | Guidance between worlds |

## Why This Works

- No Seeker character to render badly — you ARE the seeker
- POV means hands/legs/feet only — LTX does body parts fine
- Doorways are simple visual transitions LTX handles well
- The Daimon is just light in human shape — no details needed
- Worlds are pure atmosphere — LTX excels at this
- The format itself teaches the philosophy (imaginal realm, thresholds, guidance)
