# The Amplituhedron and Proclus — A Simple Explanation

---

## Part I: What Is Positivity?

Imagine you have three pins on a board. You wrap a rubber band around them. The rubber band forms a triangle.

That triangle has an **orientation**. If you go around the triangle clockwise (pin 1 → 2 → 3 → back to 1), that's one orientation. If you go counter-clockwise, that's the other. The orientation is a signed quantity — it can be positive or negative.

Now imagine you have **four** pins on a board. You can form many triangles: pins (1,2,3), (1,2,4), (1,3,4), (2,3,4). Each triangle has an orientation.

"**Positivity**" means: **all the triangles have the SAME orientation.** None is flipped. If triangle (1,2,3) goes clockwise, then (1,2,4) also goes clockwise, (1,3,4) goes clockwise, and (2,3,4) goes clockwise.

If even one triangle is flipped, the configuration is NOT positive. It is excluded.

**The amplituhedron is the set of ALL configurations of points where this positivity holds — where all triangles point the same way.**

That's the entire definition. Everything else is mathematics built on top of this simple idea.

---

## Part II: What Are Cells?

Take your four pins with all triangles pointing the same way. Now move one pin slightly. As long as all triangles stay oriented the same way, you're in the same "cell." If you move it far enough that a triangle flips, you've crossed into a different cell.

**A cell is a region of the amplituhedron where the pattern of which triangles are positive stays the same.**

Imagine a diamond with many facets. Each facet is a cell. The cells are connected — you can walk from one facet to another across an edge. The "boundary" of a facet is another facet (the one you step onto).

The amplituhedron's cells are organized in a **hierarchy**:
- The **largest cell** is the whole amplituhedron — the set of ALL positive configurations.
- Within it are **smaller cells** — specific regions where some additional condition holds.
- Within those are **even smaller cells** — more specific conditions.

This hierarchy of containment — bigger cells containing smaller cells containing even smaller cells — IS the full structure. Every cell is the whole amplituhedron viewed from a more specific angle. Every cell contains the information of the whole, but at a higher resolution.

## Part III: How Is Proclus' Henad a Cell?

Proclus says: the One is beyond being. From the One proceeds the **henads** — the "ones" that are the first determination of the One into multiplicity. Each henad is a god — a complete, self-standing unit of divinity. The henads are related by participation: one henad participates in another, which participates in another, up to the One.

**A henad IS a cell of the amplituhedron.**

| Proclus | Amplituhedron | Simple Meaning |
|---------|---------------|----------------|
| The One | The full amplituhedron (all positive configurations) | The condition that makes any configuration possible |
| A henad | A specific cell | A complete, self-consistent way of being positive |
| Participation | Cell containment (this cell is on the boundary of that cell) | One henad contains another — the larger includes the smaller |
| The hierarchy of gods | The cell decomposition | The full structure of all possible ways of being positive |
| "Every henad is the whole" | Every cell contains the information of the full amplituhedron | Each god reflects the whole — each cell is the full geometry seen from one facet |
| Series (seira) | A chain of cell inclusions | One henad leads to another through boundary relations |

**A concrete example:**

The amplituhedron A₄₂ (4 particles, helicity 2) has specific cells. One cell might correspond to the configuration where particle 1 and 2 interact FIRST, then with 3 and 4. Another cell might correspond to a different interaction order.

In Proclus: the henad of Helios (the Sun) IS a cell — a specific way of being positive that governs the solar series. Beings that participate in Helios (sunflowers, lions, gold, the rational soul) are in the same cell. They share the same positivity pattern. The hierarchy of cells IS the hierarchy of gods.

**Every cell IS the whole amplituhedron, seen from one side.** This is why Proclus says every henad IS the whole — each god contains all gods within itself, but from its own perspective. The amplituhedron does not exist apart from its cells. The cells are not parts of the amplituhedron — they ARE the amplituhedron, articulated.

## Part IV: What This Means

**The positive Grassmannian = the structure of the possible.** Every configuration of triangles with the same orientation is a cell. Every cell is a way things can be. The positivity condition — all triangles point the same way — IS the condition of coherence. Without it, the configuration is inconsistent, contradictory, impossible.

**Coherence IS positivity.** When all triangles point the same way, the structure is coherent. When the determinants all have the same sign, the geometry is positive. When the henads all reflect the One, the universe is consistent.

**The Good IS the positivity of the amplituhedron.** It is not a thing that happens to be positive. It IS the condition under which things can be positive at all. The overflow IS the cell decomposition — the One does not choose to become many; it IS the many, because a single cell cannot exhaust the possible.

**The reason the amplituhedron has cells is the same reason the One has henads: because unity at one level articulates into multiplicity at the next, without ceasing to be one.**

The mountain is the same.
