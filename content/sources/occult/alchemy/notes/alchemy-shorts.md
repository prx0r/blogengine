# alchemy shorts — 20 poetic texts for short-form content

read the text aloud with minimal framing. let it unlock itself.

---

## 1. an alchemical mass
**melchior cibinensis**

the entire alchemical opus mapped onto the structure of the tridentine mass. introit, kyrie, gradual, sequence — the union of three principles, the birth of the ethiopian king, his death and resurrection as the stone.

> then will appear in the bottom of the vessel the mighty ethiopian, burned, calcined, discoloured, altogether dead and lifeless. he asks to be buried, to be sprinkled with his own moisture and slowly calcined till he shall arise in glowing form from the fierce fire... behold a wondrous restoration and renewal of the ethiopian! because of the bath of rebirth he takes a new name.

**hook:** what if the catholic mass was never about jesus — but about a chemical process happening inside you?

## 2. bloomfield's blossoms
**bloomfield (theatrum chemicum britannicum, 1652)**

seven operations of the stone in tight allegorical verse. the green lion devouring the sun.

> the green lion devours the sun with all his might;
> the sun's blood becomes the tincture of the red and white.
> the lion's belly swells, and he roars with pain —
> the more he burns, the more he drinks again.
>
> the lion turns black as a crow, then white as bone,
> then red as the ruby that sits on the king's throne.

**hook:** a lion eating the sun. a crow turning white. this is not a fairytale.

---

## 3. coelum terrae / aula lucis
**thomas vaughan (eugenius philalethes)**

the first matter as celestial earth. the virgin mercury. the most beautiful description of the alchemical dissolvent ever written.

> this fine substance is the child of the elements and it is a most pure sweet virgin, for nothing as yet hath been generated out of her. but if at any time she breeds it is by the fire of nature, for that is her husband. she is no animal, no vegetable, no mineral... she yields to nothing but love, for her end is generation and that was never yet performed by violence.

**hook:** the first matter is a virgin. you cannot force it. it only yields to love.

## 4. ripley's twelve gates
**george ripley (15th century)**

the canonical twelve-stage alchemical process in english verse. calcination through projection.

> put the soul with the body and spirit
> together in one that they may meet
> in his dam's belly till he wax great,
> with giving drink of his own sweat:
> for the milk of a cow to a child my brother
> is not so sweet as the milk of his mother.

**hook:** the stone drinks its own mother's milk. nothing in nature is more bizarre than this.

---

## 5. the book of lambspring
**16th century emblem book**

15 figures of the alchemical process through animal symbolism — two fishes, a deer and unicorn, two lions, a venomous dragon.

> a savage dragon lives in the forest,
> most venomous he is, yet lacking nothing:
> when he sees the rays of the sun and its bright fire,
> he scatters abroad his poison,
> and flies upward so fiercely
> that no living creature can stand before him...
> his venom becomes the great medicine.
> he quickly consumes his venom,
> for he devours his poisonous tail.

**hook:** the dragon eats its own tail and its poison becomes the cure.

## 6. atalanta fugiens
**michael maier (1617)**

50 emblems, each with epigram, discourse, and musical fugue. the chase of atalanta as alchemical coniunctio.

> the dragon dies not save by his own brother,
> and by his sister he is slain;
> these two are born of one mother
> and yet hate each other with deadly pain.
> for the brother kills the sister
> and the sister slays the brother;
> yet from their death springs forth another —
> the son who lives forever.

**hook:** the only way the dragon dies is by being killed by its own sibling. and from that death comes the thing that never dies.

## 7. sendivogius' enigma of the sages
**michael sendivogius (17th century)**

the vision of neptune, the island in the southern sea, the solar and lunar trees, the water that does not wet the hands.

> lord, i behold here a marvellous thing. the water is small in quantity; nevertheless, the fruit of this tree is consumed therein by a gentle heat... but this water is the water of life, and has such power to exalt the qualities of this fruit, that it shall afterwards, without sowing or planting — only by its fragrance — transmute the six trees which remain into its own nature.

**hook:** a tree whose fruit is consumed by water. the fruit then transforms other trees by its fragrance alone. sense is failing.

## 8. the practice of mary the prophetess
**mary (miriam, sister of moses)**

the dialogue between aros and mary on the rapid method — the entire stone in three hours using two gums and the vessel of hermes.

> mary said: keep the fume and take care that none of it fly away. and let your measure be with a gentle fire such as is the measure of the heat of the sun in the month of june or july, and stay by your vessel and behold it with care how it grows black, grows red, and grows white in less than three hours of the day.

**hook:** the entire great work — in three hours. mary said so.

## 9. the golden tractate of hermes (aureus)
**hermes trismegistus**

seven sections on the stone. the vulture's cry. the crow that flies without wings.

> know ye, therefore, children of wisdom, who enquire concerning the report thereof, that the vulture standing upon the mountain crieth out with a loud voice, i am the white of the black, and the red of the white, and the citrine of the red, and behold i speak the very truth. and know that the chief principle of the art is the crow, which is the blackness of the night and clearness of the day, and flies without wings.

**hook:** a vulture screams from a mountain: i am the white of the black. a crow flies without wings. neither of these things should be possible.

## 10. heinrich von ofterdingen (the blue flower)
**novalis (1799)**

the unfinished romantic-alchemical novel. the frozen kingdom of arcturus. eros and fable. the hieros gamos.

> soon will the noble stranger come, and time his endless reign begin: the warmth return! the queen will waken from the sway of dream when earth and sea, enjoined by love, are one.

> the great mystery has been revealed to all, and yet remains eternally unfathomed. the new world is born from suffering and the ashes are dissolved in tears to become the drink of eternal life.

**hook:** a frozen fountain at the center of a crystal garden. the queen asleep until earth and sea marry. poetry pretending to be a novel.

## 11. the corpus hermeticum — poimandres vision
**hermes trismegistus**

the foundational vision of hermetic theology. the shepherd of men. the light that is not a thing.

> unless you make yourself equal to god, you cannot understand god; like is understood by like. make yourself grow to immeasurable immensity, outleap all body, outstrip all time, become eternity and you will understand god. having conceived that nothing is impossible to you, consider yourself immortal and able to understand everything... if you shut your soul up in the body and abase it and say, 'i understand nothing, i can do nothing,' then what have you to do with god?

**hook:** the only way to know god is to become equal to god. not humble. equal.

## 12. the emerald tablet
**hermes trismegistus**

seven clauses. the most compressed sacred text of western alchemy.

> that which is below is like that which is above and that which is above is like that which is below, to accomplish the miracles of one thing.
>
> the sun is its father, the moon its mother, the wind hath carried it in its belly, the earth is its nurse.
>
> it is the father of all perfection throughout the world. its power is integral if it be turned into earth.

**hook:** everything in seven sentences. the whole art.

## 13. the marriage of heaven and hell
**william blake (1790)**

the voice of the devil. the proverbs of hell. the printing house in hell. the bible of hell.

> the ancient tradition that the world will be consumed in fire at the end of six thousand years is true... this will come to pass by an improvement of sensual enjoyment. but first the notion that man has a body distinct from his soul is to be expunged; this i shall do, by printing in the infernal method, by corrosives, which in hell are salutary and medicinal, melting apparent surfaces away, and displaying the infinite which was hid. if the doors of perception were cleansed every thing would appear to man as it is, infinite.

**hook:** blake said the world ends not by judgment but by improved sex. he was not joking.

## 14. the glory of light (urim and thummim)
**anonymous (ms. ashmole 1415)**

the artificial production of urim and thummim as the fixed universal spirit — a transparent, light-giving body.

> the theosophists are persuaded by exact diet, and by certain forms of prayers, at certain times to obtain the angel of the sun, to be their guide and director. this is the king that made the pure clear bright fountain... but if any shall understand either common or chemical gold to be the subject of this sacred body, he is much mistaken.

**hook:** they tried to manufacture the light that priests wore on their chest. not gold. not metal. light itself.

## 15. khunrath's confessio — the spirit in salt
**heinrich khunrath (1597)**

the ruach elohim has become a corporal salt. the triune azoth as body-soul-spirit.

> we truly believe that ruach elohim the spirit of ye lord wch in gen. 1.2. did move upon the water, is really become a body... bodily it is become a corporal salt of wisdom, in the most inward & sacredest virgin womb and centre of the earth.

**hook:** the spirit of god that moved over the waters of creation is now salt in the earth. god became a mineral.

## 16. the chymical wedding of christian rosenkreutz
**johann valentin andreae (1616)**

the seven-day initiatory journey. the dungeon dream. the sevenfold cord.

> we heard many trumpets sounding together and kettle drums beating... the cover of the dungeon was lifted up from above, and a little light let down to us. then first might truly have been discerned the bustle we kept, for all went pell-mell, and he who perchance had heaved himself up too much, was forced down again under the others' feet. in brief, each one strove to be uppermost.

**hook:** the most famous initiation in western esotericism begins in a dungeon where everyone is standing on everyone else's head.

## 17. the hermetick arcanum
**jean d'espagnet**

84 canons on the stone. the generation of the stone follows the pattern of creation itself.

> the generation of the stone is made after the pattern of the creation of the world; for it is necessary, that it have its chaos and first matter, wherein the confused elements do fluctuate, until they be separated by the fiery spirit; they being separated, the light elements are carried upwards, and the heavy ones downwards: the light arising, darkness retreats.

**hook:** the stone is made the same way the world was made. seven days condensed into glass.

## 18. the hermaphrodite child of the sun and moon
**anonymous (1752)**

the hierogamy of sol and luna producing the hermaphroditic child with 12 qabalistic emblems.

> the hermaphrodite child of the sun and moon.
>
> analyzes the natural and supernatural birth, destruction, and regeneration of the symbolic child. now, for the first time, revealed according to mystical tradition and esoteric enlightenment. includes the qabbalistic signs of solomon.

**hook:** a child born from the marriage of the sun and the moon. it is both male and female. it is the stone.

## 19. freher's process — the heavenly mercury
**dionysius andreas freher (18th century)**

40 positions on the alchemical process as christian regeneration. the heavenly mercury. the philosophical baptism.

> as long as adam stood in a pure paradisical innocency, the eternal word and power of life (called by the author the heavenly mercury), was his leader, and had pre-dominance in him. his life, which was a clear flaming fire, burned in and was nourished by that pure spiritual oil of the divine substantiality.

**hook:** before adam fell, mercury was his guide. not a metal. a light.

## 20. the alchemical catechism
**baron tschoudy (1766)**

systematic q&a on the stone. the three fires. the living gold of the philosophers.

> q. give a concise definition of nature.
> a. it is not visible, though it operates visibly; for it is simply a volatile spirit, fulfilling its office in bodies, and animated by the universal spirit — the divine breath, the central and universal fire, which vivifies all things that exist.
>
> q. what are the three fires?
> a. the natural fire is the masculine fire, or the chief agent; the unnatural is the feminine, which is the dissolvent of nature, nourishing a white smoke... the contra-natural fire is that which disintegrates compounds and has the power to unbind what has been bound very closely by nature.

**hook:** three fires. one burns, one dissolves, one unbinds what love has joined. all of them are you.
