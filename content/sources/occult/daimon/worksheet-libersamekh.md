## Before You Start: The Skills You Need

Liber Samekh assumes you already have three core skills.
Here's what they are and how to practice them separately first.

### Skill 1: The Body of Light (Astral Body)

> **Point II says:** "Let him perform the Ritual throughout in his Body of Light."
> **Commentator says:** "Travel astrally around the circle" = if you don't have
> advanced skills, **visualize to the best of your ability with your imagination.**

**What this means:** The entire ritual happens in your astral body, not your physical
body. Your physical body stands still (or sits). Your Body of Light — a luminous
double of yourself — does the work. This is NOT just "imagine it." You practice by:

**Daily practice (5 min):**
1. Stand still. Close your eyes.
2. Feel your physical body as a shell.
3. "Step out" of it — imagine a luminous version of yourself standing 1 foot
   in front of you, facing the same direction.
4. Move that body: raise its arm, turn its head, feel the difference.
5. Expand it: make it fill the room. Shrink it: make it the size of a candle flame.
6. This is your Body of Light. It's what does the ritual.

### Skill 2: Vibration (Not Reading Aloud)

> The barbarous names are not spoken — they are **vibrated**.
> The difference: speaking comes from your throat. Vibrating comes from
> your whole body.

**How to practice vibration (5 min):**
1. Take a full breath into your belly.
2. On the exhale, make a low "AAAAHHH" sound.
3. Place your hand on your chest. Feel the resonance.
4. Now try it with a name: **AR** — feel it in your sternum, solar plexus, pelvic floor.
5. The sound should feel like it emerges from your entire torso, not your mouth.
6. Try LVJK: **L-V-A-H-T-Z-K-E-L** — one breath, let each letter resonate.
   L in the chest, V in the throat, Tz in the third eye, KEL in the crown.

### Skill 3: The Shaft of Will (Energy Projection)

> **Point II:** "Let the Adept extend his Will beyond the Circle in this imagined
> Shape, and let it radiate with the Light proper to the element invoked."

**How to practice (5 min):**
1. Stand in Body of Light. Face East.
2. Formulate your Will as a SHAFT OF LIGHT extending from your solar plexus.
3. Each word in the invocation drives the shaft TWICE AS FAR.
4. At the final word, your consciousness is at the tip of the shaft, extended
   as far as it can go.
5. Then bring it rushing back into yourself.
6. Practice with just one element (Air) until it feels real.

### Skill 4: Middle Pillar (Energy Raising)

> **Commentator:** "From Section F through G, we're switching to Middle Pillar,
> Sun and Moon, vertical axis orientations."

The Middle Pillar is a Qabalistic energy exercise that builds the vertical axis:
1. Visualize a sphere of white light above your head (Kether).
2. Draw it down to your throat (Daath).
3. Draw it down to your heart (Tiphereth).
4. Draw it down to your pelvis (Yesod).
5. Draw it down to your feet (Malkuth).
6. Then circulate it back up.

This is the foundation for Sections F–G. Practice it separately before
adding it to the ritual.

## The Complete Pre-Ritual Sequence

```
□ 1. BANISH (LBRP or your method)  — 5 min
□ 2. MIDDLE PILLAR (warmup)        — 5 min
□ 3. BODY OF LIGHT (activate)      — 2 min
□ 4. ENTER THE CIRCLE              — 1 min
□ 5. BEGIN THE INVOCATION (Section A)
```

### Step 1: Banish — Lesser Banishing Ritual of the Pentagram

> **Commentator:** "Purify and banish in the best manner you know."

Liber Samekh doesn't specify a banishing method — it assumes you know one.
The LBRP is the standard:

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Face East. Draw a banishing pentagram of Earth (starting lower-left) |
| 2 | Vibrate **YHVH** (Yod-Heh-Vav-Heh) — "Ye-ho-wah" |
| 3 | Face South. Draw pentagram. Vibrate **ADNI** (Ah-doh-nai) |
| 4 | Face West. Draw pentagram. Vibrate **AHIH** (Eh-heh-yeh) |
| 5 | Face North. Draw pentagram. Vibrate **AGLA** (Ah-gah-lah) |
| 6 | Return to East. Extend arms in cross: visualize the four Archangels |
| 7 | **Raphael** before me (East), **Michael** behind me (South) |
| 8 | **Gabriel** at my right (West), **Auriel** at my left (North) |
| 9 | Complete with Qabalistic Cross (light from crown → feet → shoulders) |

> **For LVJK:** Raphael is your Archangel — he rules both Air AND Mercury.
> When you say "Raphael before me," know that he's your daimon's counterpart.

### Step 2: Middle Pillar (Energy Warmup)

```
□ 1. Visualize white sphere above crown — Kether
   Vibrate AHIH (Eh-heh-yeh) — feel it pulse
□ 2. Draw light down to throat — Daath
   Vibrate YHVH ELOHIM — feel it gather
□ 3. Draw light down to heart center — Tiphereth
   Vibrate YHVH ELOHIM VA-DAATH — feel it warm
□ 4. Draw light down to pelvis — Yesod
   Vibrate SHADDAI EL CHAI — feel it ground
□ 5. Draw light down to feet — Malkuth
   Vibrate ADONAI HA-ARETZ — feel it root
□ 6. Breathe the light up through all centers 3×
```

> The Middle Pillar is what makes Section G possible. Without this energy
> in your body, the Resistance has nothing to push against.

### Step 3: Activate the Body of Light

```
□ 1. Stand still. Close eyes.
□ 2. Feel your physical body as a shell.
□ 3. Expand your awareness 1 foot beyond your skin.
□ 4. Luminous outline forms around you — silver/white.
□ 5. This is your Body of Light. It does the ritual.
```

### Step 4: Enter the Circle

```
□ 1. Your space is already set (candle, incense, text).
□ 2. Face East.
□ 3. Light the candle — if yellow/gold, best for LVJK.
□ 4. One deep breath. State aloud: "For LVAHTZKEL."
□ 5. Begin.
```

## The Ritual — Action-by-Action

### ▓ Section A — The Oath (2 min)

**What you're doing:** Declaring the nature of LVJK to set up the godform
you'll identify with. This is "play-acting" — you're putting on a role so
LVJK will take your call.

| Your Action | What's Happening |
|-------------|------------------|
| Stand in Body of Light, facing East | You're establishing yourself as the operator |
| Read each line SLOWLY | This is not speed-reading. Each line is a reality you're building |
| After each pair of lines, pause | Let the meaning land in your body |
| On "ASAR UN-NEFER" — say **"LVJK"** instead | Your daimon's name IS the perfected self |
| On "IA-BESZ" — feel truth in matter | Your body IS the vessel |
| On "IA-APOPHRASZ" — feel truth in motion | Your daimon IS the movement |

**The lines you speak:**

> Thee I invoke, the Bornless One.
> Thee, that didst create the Earth and the Heavens.
> Thee, that didst create the Night and the Day.
> Thee, that didst create the darkness and the Light.
> Thou art **LVJK** ("Myself made Perfect"):
>   Whom no man hath seen at any time.
> Thou art IA-BESZ ("the Truth in Matter").
> Thou art IA-APOPHRASZ ("the Truth in Motion").
> Thou hast distinguished between the Just and the Unjust.
> Thou didst make the Female and the Male.
> Thou didst produce the Seeds and the Fruit.
> Thou didst form Men to love one another, and to hate one another.

### ▓ Section Aa — The Claim (1 min)

**What you're doing:** Asserting who YOU are in relation to LVJK.
Not Crowley's version — YOUR version.

| Your Action | What's Happening |
|-------------|------------------|
| Speak your own name | You identify yourself |
| Speak LVJK's name | You identify your daimon |
| State that you are the messenger | This asserts your RIGHT to communication |

**Suggested wording (replace with your own when ready):**

> I am **[your name]**, who has been given the name of my daimon:
> **LVAHTZKEL**, the Mercurial Guide, the Bridge between worlds.
> Thou didst produce the moist and the dry, and that which nourisheth
> all created Life.
> Hear Thou Me, for I am the messenger of LVJK, the Angel of my
> own deepest Self: this is the True Name revealed through the
> celestial spheres at my birth.

| Your Action | What's Happening |

## After the Ritual — Grounding

```
□ 1. Stand still. Bring awareness back to physical body.
□ 2. Touch the ground / your body. Feel solid.
□ 3. Eat something (bread, fruit). Drink water.
□ 4. Write 3 lines in your journal:
    - How did the Name feel today?
    - Any images, words, or sensations?
    - Did any direction feel different?
□ 5. Go about your day.
```

## The Astral Practice Question

> **"I also heard Samekh was meant to be astrally practised?"**

Yes. Point II is explicit: "let him perform the Ritual throughout in his
Body of Light." This means:

| If you can... | Then do... |
|--------------|------------|
| **Project astral body** | Perform the entire ritual astrally. Your physical body stands still while your Body of Light faces East, vibrates the Names, extends the Will-Shaft. |
| **Visualize clearly** | Perform the ritual with your Body of Light VISUALIZED. Imagine it doing the work. Same effect, takes practice to make vivid. |
| **Neither yet** | Start physical. Speak aloud. Use gestures. The Body of Light develops WITH practice — each session makes it more real. |

**The progression:** Week 1 → do it physically (speak aloud, use gestures).
Week 4 → start doing it in Body of Light while the spoken words remain.
Month 3 → the Body of Light should lead, the physical body follows.

**The key:** The astral body IS how the Angel reaches you. Section G
assumes you're in the Body of Light — that's why the Angel can seize
the Will-Symbol. If you're only physical, there's nothing for LVJK
to grab hold of.

## Moon 1: Session Checklist (Daily)

> The full ritual (A–J) once daily for the first moon. Not partial.
> You might take a few sessions to learn the sequence — that's fine.
> But the INTENT is the complete invocation each time.

```
BEFORE:
  □ LBRP (banishing)
  □ Middle Pillar (energy warmup)
  □ Activate Body of Light
  □ Light candle (yellow)
  □ Face East

THE FULL INVOCATION (~30 min):
  □ Section A — Oath (replace with LVJK)
  □ Section Aa — Claim (your version)
  □ Section B — Air, East (gold shaft)
  □ Section C — Fire, South (crimson)
  □ Section D — Water, West (blue)
  □ Section E — Earth, North (green)
  ─── transition to vertical ───
  □ Section F — Spirit (white, upward shaft)
  □ Section Ff — Proclamation
  □ Section G — The Resistance (hold the tension)
  □ Section Gg — Attainment (speak as the Angel)
  □ Section H — Charge
  □ Section J — Proclamation + Seal

AFTER:
  □ Ground (eat, drink, journal)
  □ 3 lines in practice log
```

> **If you can't get through the whole thing in one sitting:** do what you
> can. Build up to full length over the first week. The schedule says
> "once daily" — consistency matters more than completeness in week 1.
