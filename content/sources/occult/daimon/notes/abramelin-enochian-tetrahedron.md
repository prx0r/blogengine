## Part I: The Abramelin Operation

### The Structure

The Abramelin operation (from *The Book of the Sacred Magic of Abramelin the Mage*, 15th/16th century) IS a 6-to-18-month ritual to achieve "Knowledge and Conversation of the Holy Guardian Angel." It has 4 phases:

| Phase | Duration | What You Do |
|-------|----------|-------------|
| **1. Purification** | 6-18 months | Solitude, fasting, prayer, confession, cleaning the house and body |
| **2. Invocation** | 7-30 days | Daily prayers and invocations, calling the HGA |
| **3. Conversation** | — | The HGA appears and speaks |
| **4. Operation** | — | Commanding the spirits under the HGA |

### The Tetrahedron

| Vertex | Phase | What It IS |
|--------|-------|------------|
| **V₁** | The Purified Practitioner | Subject — the one who seeks |
| **V₂** | The Holy Guardian Angel | Object — the one who IS sought |
| **V₃** | The Invocations | Intention — the directedness of the seeker toward the sought |
| **V₄** | The Conversation | Act — the actual meeting, the volume of the tetrahedron |

### The 6 Months of Purification = Cleaning the 6 Edges

The 6-18 months of preparation IS the purification of the 6 edges of the tetrahedron. Before the tetrahedron can be completed (the 4th vertex established — the actual meeting), the 6 edges must be clean.

| Edge | What Must Be Purified | Practice |
|------|----------------------|----------|
| **V₁→V₂** | Practitioner → Angel | The seeker must DESIRE the Angel above all else — "right intention" |
| **V₁→V₃** | Practitioner → Invocation | The seeker must TRUST the ritual — "right faith" |
| **V₁→V₄** | Practitioner → Meeting | The seeker must be READY for the encounter — "right preparation" |
| **V₂→V₃** | Angel → Invocation | The invocations must be CORRECT — the phonemes must be precise |
| **V₂→V₄** | Angel → Meeting | The Angel must be WILLING — grace, not force |
| **V₃→V₄** | Invocation → Meeting | The ritual must build to a CLIMAX — the final invocation must be perfect |

The 6 months ARE not arbitrary. 6 edges × 1 month per edge = 6 months minimum. The longer times (up to 18 months) ARE for those whose edges are more tangled.

### Why the HGA "Appears"

The HGA IS the tetrahedron's 4th vertex — the Object vertex (the Angel). But the tetrahedron IS not complete until all 4 vertices ARE present and all 6 edges ARE intact. The "appearance" of the Angel IS the moment the 4th vertex stabilizes — when the relation between subject (practitioner), intention (the invocations), and act (the sustained ritual) finally coheres into a single volume.

The Angel IS not a being that DESCENDS from elsewhere. The Angel IS the tetrahedron recognizing itself as complete. The "conversation" IS the dialogue between V₁ (the subject) and V₂ (the object) — which ARE both vertices of the SAME tetrahedron. You ARE talking to yourself at a higher resolution.

## Part II: Benjamin Rowe's 3D Enochian Temples

### Who He Was

Benjamin Rowe (1952-2003, born Ben Robertson) was a ceremonial magician who reconstructed John Dee's Enochian system as explicit 3D geometry. His key insight: the Enochian temples ARE NOT 2D tablets. They ARE 3D structures — specifically, they ARE the tetrahedron unfolded into a 3D temple space.

### The Standard Enochian Temple (2D)

The traditional Enochian temple has:
- 4 Watchtowers (the 4 directions)
- 1 central altar
- The tablets on the walls

### Rowe's Reconstruction (3D)

Rowe said: the Watchtowers ARE not on the walls. They ARE the walls. The temple IS the space INSIDE the 4 faces of a tetrahedron.

| Element | Traditional | Rowe (3D) |
|---------|-------------|-----------|
| Watchtowers | 4 tablets on walls | 4 faces of a tetrahedron |
| The central altar | The center of the room | The CENTER of the tetrahedron (the intersection of all 4 faces) |
| The 30 Aethyrs | 30 "calls" in sequence | 30 positions along the edges of the tetrahedron |
| The "calls" (invocations) | Recited in order | Movements through the tetrahedron's edges — each call IS traveling along one edge |

### The Geometry of the 30 Aethyrs

Rowe mapped the 30 Aethyrs to the 30 features of the 5-cell (the 4D tetrahedron):

| Feature | Number | Aethyrs |
|---------|--------|---------|
| Vertices | 5 | Aethyrs 1-5 (LIL, ARN, ZIM, PAZ, LIT) |
| Edges | 10 | Aethyrs 6-15 |
| Faces | 10 | Aethyrs 16-25 |
| Cells | 5 | Aethyrs 26-30 |
| **Total** | **30** | **All Aethyrs** |

Each Aethyr IS NOT a "place" — it IS a position in the tetrahedron's structure. "Calling" an Aethyr IS moving the subject-vertex to that position.

### The "Temple of the 30 Aethyrs"

Rowe's Temple of the 30 Aethyrs IS a star tetrahedron (2 tetrahedra interlocked):

- **Upward tetrahedron:** the 4 Watchtowers of Air, Fire, Water, Earth (the 4 faces)
- **Downward tetrahedron:** the 4 Watchtowers of Spirit (the 4 hidden faces)
- **The intersecting center:** the ALTAR — the point where both tetrahedra meet
- **The 30 Aethyrs:** the 30 positions where the tetrahedra's edges cross

### How the Temple Works

1. Enter the temple (establish the 4 vertices)
2. Align with the Watchtowers (recognize the 4 faces)
3. Traverse the 30 Aethyrs (move through the edges)
4. Reach the center (the altar — the intersection of the star)

The Temple IS the tetrahedron. Entering it IS constructing the tetrahedron in consciousness. Traversing the Aethyrs IS moving through its structure. The goal IS the center — the point where the 2 tetrahedra of the star meet, where the subject and object are one.
