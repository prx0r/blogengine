# Daimon Contact — The 8-Week Practice Path

For Lvahtzkel (LVJK / לוצכל). Mercurial daimon. Day birth.

---

## Overview

8 weeks. 4 phases. 8 spells — each a checkpoint that requires more
purification and skill than the last. Complete a spell before advancing.

| Phase | Weeks | Spell Checkpoint | Skill Developed |
|-------|-------|-----------------|-----------------|
| 1 | 1-2 | Prayer to the Good Daimon + Dream revelation | Voice + dream recall |
| 2 | 3-4 | Phylactery + Lunar invocation + Bowl divination | Timing + purity + concentration |
| 3 | 5-6 | Systasis to Helios + Rite of driving out | Authority + hourly signs |
| 4 | 7-8 | Meeting your own daimon + Assistant daimon | Full contact |

---

## How This Works

Frater Acher's doctor-camera metaphor: The daimon is the surgeon. You are
the camera. The camera doesn't need to *talk* to the surgeon. It needs to
stop acting like it has a will of its own and simply transmit clearly.

You don't summon a daimon. You become capable of perceiving one.

> "Not as if my angel had 'appeared' — but much more as if I had finally,
> after decades of blindness, managed to look above my own head."
> — Frater Acher, *The Holy Daimon*

He named his daimon Pollux. His final lesson:
> "It does not require a path; it requires acceptance."

## Optional Skill-Builders (from Acher's Trust Stage)

If a spell checkpoint is blocking you, these exercises develop the
specific skill you're missing:

**Open hand / tight fist:** Hold left hand completely open. Right hand
in a tight fist. Hold both simultaneously. Feel yourself as the bridge
between them. This teaches holding polarities without merging them —
essential for the resistance practice in Phase 3.

**Tratak (focused):** Gaze at a single candle flame without blinking.
Close eyes and hold the negative imprint. Develops the sustained focus
needed for bowl divination (Spell 5).

**Tratak (peripheral):** Two candles at edges of your visual field.
Hold both simultaneously without looking directly at either. Develops
the "soft gaze" needed to perceive the daimon "from behind" (Spell 8).

**The serpent and the egg:** This is Frater Acher's core preparation for
the systasis. See the full 7-day practice under "Systasis Preparation"
before Phase 4. Do it in the week leading up to the crescent moon.

## Core Daily Practice (all phases)

**Dawn (20 min):**
1. Wash face, hands, feet — cold water
2. Stand facing east, window open, candle lit
3. 7 deep breaths
4. Sing LVAHTZKEL 7× — one exhale each, let it vibrate in the chest
5. 10 min silence — do nothing, expect nothing

**Dusk (10 min):**
1. Light candle
2. Read what you wrote this morning
3. 5 min silence
4. Extinguish candle

**Journal:** Write 3 lines each morning immediately after practice.
Date. How the name felt. Any images/words/feelings that came.

## Phase 1 — Foundation (Weeks 1-2)

### Spell 1: Prayer to the Good Daimon
PGM VII.644-651. No materials needed. Just voice.

*Evening, 3× this week:*
> Hail, Good Daimon, Lord of all things visible and invisible.
> Agathos Daimon, benevolent helper of mankind,
> I invoke Thy holy and incorruptible names.
> Come to me, great Spirit. Be present in this space.

Say it aloud. Then sit 5 min in silence and notice what changes.

**Pass condition:** You feel a distinct shift in the room's
atmosphere when you say the prayer.

### Spell 2: Dream Revelation
PGM VII.478-490. Requires dream recall.

*Nightly, before sleep:*
1. On a strip of paper, write: ACHACHAEL CHACHOU MARMARIOUTI
2. Wear it around your neck or place under pillow
3. Say once:
   > Eros, darling PASSALEON ET, send me my personal angel tonight
   > to give me information about whatever concerns me.
   > I do this on order from PAN-CHOUCHI THASSOU.
4. Sleep. In morning, write all dream fragments immediately.

**Pass condition:** You recall a dream that feels like a response.

## Phase 2 — Invocation (Weeks 3-4)

### Spell 3: Phylactery
PGM IV.86-87. Basic talismanic.

*Day 1 of this phase:*
Write the names of protection on a small square of clean paper.
Wear it on your body for the duration of this phase.
Renew if it gets damaged or wet.

This creates a bounded space for the work that follows.
Without it, the higher invocations can scatter your energy.

### Spell 4: Lunar Invocation
PGM VII.862-918. Requires timing.

*First night of the waxing moon:*
1. Stand in moonlight (or open window facing the moon)
2. Burn frankincense
3. Say the lunar prayer seven times:
   > I call on you, Lady Isis, whom Agathos Daimon permitted
   > to rule in the entire black land.
   > LOU LOULOU BATHARTHAR THARESIBATH ATHERNEKLESICH
   > ATHERNEBOUNI EICHOMO CHOMOTHI Isis Sothis,
   > SOUERI, Boubastis, EURELIBAT CHAMARI NEBOUTOS OUERI
   > AIE EOA OAI. Protect me, great and marvelous names.
4. Sit in moonlight 10 min

**Pass condition:** You feel the moon as a living presence, not
just a symbol.

### Spell 5: Bowl Divination
PGM IV.154-285 (simplified). Requires concentration.

*Once, mid-phase:*
1. Fill a dark bowl with water
2. Light one candle behind it
3. Gaze at the water's surface — not at reflections, *through*
4. Recite LVJK softly, letting the name blur into the water
5. Hold for 10 min without looking away or blinking
6. Note any images that formed in the water or in your mind

**Pass condition:** You see something in the water that isn't a
reflection of the room.

## Phase 3 — Threshold (Weeks 5-6)

### Spell 6: Systasis to Helios
PGM III.494-611. Requires knowledge of the solar hours.

*Pick a clear day. Perform at sunrise:*
1. Face east. Know the form of Helios for this hour (hour 1 = young
   monkey, silver fir tree, stone aphanos, name PHROUER).
2. Say:
   > Come to me from the four winds of the world,
   > air-transversing, great god.
   > Hear me in every ritual which I perform,
   > and grant all the petitions of my prayer completely,
   > because I know your signs, symbols, and forms,
   > who you are each hour and what your name is.
3. Recite the correct name for this hour from the 12-hour cycle.
4. Feel the sun respond — a warmth, a pressure, a light behind the eyes.

**Pass condition:** The invocation produces a tangible physical
sensation — heat, pressure, or light.

### Spell 7: Rite for Driving Out
PGM IV.1872-1927. Requires authority.

*Once, at a Saturn hour:*
This is not for exorcising demons. It's for clearing the space of
any lingering energies that might distort your daimon contact.
Perform it once as a purification of your practice area.

**Pass condition:** The space feels empty and clean afterward.

## Systasis Preparation — The Egg Practice

Frater Acher's three exercises for the week before the systasis.
Do these daily in the 7 days leading up to the crescent moon.

**Day 1: Break an egg.**
Take a raw egg. Hold it in your hand. Feel its weight. Think about
what it means to break something that could have held life. Then throw
it on the floor. Sit with the mess for five minutes before cleaning.
This teaches you that you have the power to destroy. The systasis
requires you to crack the egg of your old self. You need to know
you're capable of it.

**Days 2-6: Carry an egg.**
Hard-boil an egg. Carry it in your hand everywhere — all day, every
touch, every task, with this one hand. Do not break it. People will
ask. Say "I'm doing a mindfulness course." This teaches the sacrifice
of protecting new life. The egg in the systasis is not just food —
it's a vessel for divine fire. You learn to hold a vessel sacred.

**Day 7: The egg and the flame.**
Hard-boil an egg. Light a candle. Hold the egg in your right hand,
your right hand extended toward the candle. In vision, pull the flame
into the egg until it glows. Hold it for 5 minutes. This is a dry run
of the charging stage. If you can't make the egg glow in vision, your
imagination needs more work before the real thing.

On the night before the systasis, review: you have broken an egg (you
can destroy), you have carried an egg (you can protect), you have charged
an egg (you can fill a vessel with fire). The ritual egg will be all three.

Liber Samekh says: "The Egg (or Heart) girt with a Serpent is a cognate
symbol... Thy wand to all Cups, and thy Disk to all Swords, **but betray
not thine Egg.**" The egg is your vessel. The serpent is the daimon.
The week of egg practice ensures your vessel is intact when it arrives.

## Phase 4 — Contact (Weeks 7-8)

### Spell 8: Meeting Your Own Daimon
PGM VII.505-528 — the Systasis.

*Crescent moon. Dawn. Mercury hour. Full ritual with egg:*

1. **Skinning:** 6 stones, one for each layer of identity. Roll each.
   Release fate, place, hour, day, time, the All.

2. **Purification:** Write Helios name on egg: AŌ SATHREN ABRASAX
   IAŌAI AEŌ ĒŌA ŌAĒ IAO IĒO EY IE IAŌAI. Rub over body.
   Lick ink off. Crack. Empty outside.

3. **Charging:** Take second egg. Pull sun-fire into it in vision.
   Speak the Helios invocation 7×.

4. **Inflaming:** Crack egg. Swallow raw. Fire moves to solar plexus.

5. **Communion:** Speak LVAHTZKEL once. Turn awareness behind you.
   Receive.

### Spell 9 (Optional): Acquiring an Assistant Daimon
PGM I.1-42. Only after successful systasis.

This establishes a permanent working relationship with the daimon.
Not needed if the systasis was clear. Useful if you want a structured
ongoing practice.

## Emergency Grounding

- Overwhelmed? Touch earth. Bare feet on soil.
- Disoriented? Eat root vegetables. Say your name aloud 3×.
- Can't sleep? Skip the dream seeding. Rest is more important.
- Fear? The daimon cannot harm you. It is you, in your highest form.
