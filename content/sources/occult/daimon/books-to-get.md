### Priority 3 — Modern Scholarship

| Text | Why | Where to look |
|------|-----|---------------|
| **The Last Pagans of Iraq** (Hämeen-Anttila) | The Harranian Sabians — the last Hellenistic pagan community, who preserved Hermetic and Neoplatonic practices into the Islamic period. Directly relevant to the transmission of daimon contact practices. | Academic press (Brill) — expensive |
| **Techniques of Greco-Egyptian Magic** (Skinner) | Modern scholarly analysis of the PGM ritual structure. Useful for understanding the mechanics behind spells we already have. | Amazon, used |
| **Techniques of Solomonic Magic** (Skinner) | Companion volume, covers the grimoire tradition. | Amazon, used |
| **Sexual Magic** (Randolph) | The foundational text on sexual current in Western occultism — relevant to Liber Samekh Section G. | Archive.org, used |
| **Bardon — The Practice of Magical Evocation (Bk 2)** | ✅ Already sourced (8.4 MB PDF in contemporary/) |

| **A Suggestive Inquiry into the Hermetic Mystery** (Atwood) | Classic of spiritual alchemy. Not freely available as real PDF. | Used bookstores |
