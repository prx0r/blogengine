# Calculating Your Daimon / Holy Guardian Angel Name

## Method: Agrippa's Genius Calculation

Source: Heinrich Cornelius Agrippa, *Three Books of Occult Philosophy*,
Book III, Chapter XXVI (and Book IV, spurious).

Restored and popularized by Frater Acher in *The Holy Daimon* (2018).

### What You Need

1. Your complete birth data: date, time, and location
2. A natal chart calculated for **Sidereal** zodiac (not tropical)
3. The spreadsheet: `tools/theomagica's Genius & Evil Daimon Calculator.xlsx`

### Method (Simplified)

Agrippa's method extracts the name of your Holy Genius (the daimon)
from the celestial configuration at your birth:

1. **Calculate the Lot of Fortune** (or your natal chart)
2. **Identify the Almuten Figuris** — the planet with the highest essential
   dignity in the Lot of Fortune's position
3. **The Almuten's name, when converted through Hebrew letter
   correspondences, yields the first syllable of your Genius name**
4. **Repeat for the Sun, Moon, Ascendant, and the Prenatal Syzygy**
   (the lunation before birth) to assemble the full name

The spreadsheet automates all of this.

### Alternative: PGM Method (Systasis)

If you don't have your natal data or prefer a ritual approach, the
PGM systasis method (PGM VII.505-528) does not require a pre-calculated
name. The daimon reveals itself through the ritual. See `spells/pgm/pgm-vii-505-528.json`.

### How to Use the Calculator

1. Open `tools/theomagica's Genius & Evil Daimon Calculator.xlsx`
2. Enter your birth data in the input fields
3. The spreadsheet calculates your Genius name from the planetary positions
4. Note that this gives the **outer** name — the inner name is revealed
   through direct contact

### Important Notes

- The calculated name is a starting point for invocation, not the
  daimon itself
- Different astrological systems (Whole Sign vs. Placidus houses,
  Tropical vs. Sidereal zodiac) yield different names
- Frater Acher recommends Sidereal zodiac for the calculation
- The name should be vibrated/sung, not just read
- The daimon may correct or replace the name during direct contact
- The theomagica spreadsheet also calculates your "Evil Daimon" name —
  this corresponds to the shadow aspect, not an independent entity

| Latin | Hebrew | Name | Value |
|-------|--------|------|-------|
| l | ל | Lamed | 30 |
| v | ו | Waw | 6 |
| j | צ | Sadhe | 90 |
| k | כ | Kaph | 20 |

So the name LVJK in Hebrew is **לוצכ** — Lamed-Vav-Sadhe-Kaph.

Note: This is a constructed magical name, not a Hebrew word with meaning.
The letters are determined by the zodiac degrees of your hylegical places
(Sun, Moon, Ascendant, Part of Fortune, Prenatal Syzygy) mapped through
