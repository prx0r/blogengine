# Corpus Hermeticum — 5 RO Breakdown Plan

The existing RO (`ro-corpus-hermeticum/ro.json`) has 25 passages covering CH I–XVII + Asclepius. For full expounding, split into 5 focused ROs:

## RO 1: Poimandres (CH I)
**Title:** The Vision of Poimandres — Creation, Descent, and Ascent
**Status:** ~80% of content exists in current RO
**Covers:**
- The vision of the light (p_001)
- Creation hymn — Nous begets the craftsman (p_002)
- Descent of Anthropos — man breaks through the vault (p_003)
- Ascent through the seven zones (p_004)
- Tetrahedron mapping for the descent/ascent arc (p_021, p_025)
- Connection to the 36 tattvas descent (p_024)

## RO 2: The Krater and Rebirth (CH IV, XIII)
