# Full Daimon Corpus — Theoretical Foundation

> Moved from practice-path.md to keep practice path pure.

Every tradition we've collected says something slightly different.
Here's what they agree on:

**Plato (Apology, Phaedo, Republic):** The daimonion is a sign that
only says "no." It never compels. It restrains. This is the Socratic
foundation — the daimon as discriminative faculty, not a being that
gives you content but one that checks your direction.
*→ Your practice is as much about learning to feel the "no" as the "yes."*

**Plutarch (De Genio Socratis):** The daimon is a higher-order
perception — like a musician hearing a wrong note. It doesn't speak
in words. It registers as a feeling of alignment or misalignment.
*→ The resistance practice trains this. You learn to feel the tension.*

**Apuleius (De Deo Socratis):** Daimons are *genere, loco, natura
medii* — intermediate in kind, place, and nature. They bridge the
mortal and divine. Your personal daimon is one of three tiers:
divine daimons, personal genii, and punishing daimons.
*→ Your daimon is specifically the second tier — your personal genius.*

**Iamblichus (De Mysteriis):** The daimon is a superior being that
cannot be reached by intellect alone. Ritual is required. The
prayer is not a petition — it's an ontological ascent. You don't
ask the daimon to come down. You raise yourself to its level.
*→ This is why the pentagrams and vibration matter. They raise you.*

**Plotinus (Enneads III.4):** The daimon is not fixed — it's the
next-higher principle above wherever you currently are. As you
evolve, your daimon changes. It's not a static entity assigned at
birth but a dynamic function of your soul's current level.
*→ If LVJK feels different in month 6 than month 1, that's correct.*

**Proclus (In Tim., In Remp.):** The daimons form a complete
hierarchy from matter to the One. Your personal daimon is the
*apoplērōtēs* — the fulfiller of your choices (Myth of Er, 620e).
It doesn't choose for you. It executes what you choose.
*→ The resistance practice is you choosing. The daimon fulfills.*

**Synesius (De Insomniis):** The daimon communicates through the
imagination (*phantasia*) and the subtle body (*pneuma*). Dreams
are the natural channel. Training the imagination through waking
practice improves dream reception.
*→ The dusk dream-seeding and the journal work are not optional.
They're the daimon's primary communication channel.*

**Psellus (De Operatione Daemonum):** Daimons have no bodies of
their own — they condense air, fire, water, or earth to interact
with the physical world. Incense, lamps, and consecrated objects
provide the media they need.
*→ The candle, the incense, the circle — these aren't decoration.
They're the medium your daimon uses to reach you.*

**Corbin (Man of Light):** The daimon is your Perfect Nature
(*Tabiat-e Tammah*) — the celestial counterpart that has been
with you since before birth. The relationship is *unus-ambo*:
1×1, not 1=1. Two that are one without ceasing to be two.
*→ The systasis communion is not merging. It's recognizing.*

**The Chaldean Oracles (via Proclus):** "The soul, having received
the fire of intellect, is empowered to act." The Oracles describe
contact as a fire that "darts and flashes through all the Depths
of the Universe." This is the verification standard.
*→ If you don't feel the fire, keep going. When you do, you'll know.*
