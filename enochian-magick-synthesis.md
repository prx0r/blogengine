## V. Why Dee and Kelley Got Different Results

Edward Kelley, the skryer (seer), produced vivid angelic visions. John Dee, the scholar, recorded and organized them. The two roles correspond to:
- **Kelley:** The **intuitive/creative** mode — accessing the 30 Aethyrs through altered states
- **Dee:** The **rational/analytical** mode — encoding the Aethyrs into mathematical tables

The collaboration produced the complete system. Dee could not see the visions. Kelley could not encode the math. The system REQUIRES both modes — which is why it has never been replicated exactly by a single practitioner.

**The Enochian system only "works" when both hemispheres of the brain are engaged.** The right hemisphere (Kelley's role) accesses the altered states. The left hemisphere (Dee's role) creates the structure. The tables are the left hemisphere's encoding of the right hemisphere's vision.
