## FABRICATIONS, ERRORS, AND QUESTIONABLE CLAIMS

1. **"3⁴ = 81 = number of stable elements in the periodic table"** (Art. 45): There are 94 naturally occurring elements, ~80 stable - approximate but presented as exact fact.

2. **"432 the only whole number when squared comes into .01% accuracy measuring the speed of light"** (Art. 41, 47): 432² = 186,624; official speed of light = 186,282 miles/sec. The .01% claim is exaggerated (actual difference ~0.18%).

3. **"Planck's constant is 6.626, which is very close to 6.66"** (Art. 47): While mathematically true, implying numerological significance to Planck's constant via 2/3 of 10 is not standard physics.

4. **"The gravitational constant is also close to 6.66"** (Art. 47): 6.674 is indeed close to 6.666.

5. **"Only the numbers 5 and 6 retain their original number when multiplied"** (Implied in Art. 47): 5×5=25 ends in 5; 6×6=36 ends in 6. But this is also true of any number ending in 5 or 6 (e.g., 25×25=625).

6. **"7920 protons and neutrons are used by 64 codons that make up our DNA"** (Art. 39): This is from Anthony Morris and is not standard biochemistry.

7. **"The electron has been said to have no mass but only a field"** (Art. 122): Electrons have a known rest mass (9.109×10⁻³¹ kg). This claim misrepresents field theory.

8. **"Neutrons do not play a role in the atom as commonly taught"** (Art. 130): This contradicts established nuclear physics. Neutrons are essential for nuclear stability.

9. **"The strong force is nothing more than gravity"** (Art. 130): This is contradicted by all experimental evidence. The strong force is ~10³⁸ times stronger than gravity at nuclear scales.

10. **Planck scale cut-off as "renormalization" denial** (Art. 138): Renormalization is a mathematical technique, not a denial of infinite spectrum. The Planck scale is a limit of known physics, not an imposed cut-off.

11. **"The universe is non-expanding" / "No Big Bang"** (Art. 80): Contradicts the overwhelming body of cosmological evidence (CMB, redshift-distance relation, Hubble's law, nucleosynthesis).

12. **"The nucleus of an atom spins at 1,022 Hz per second"** (Art. 130): Nuclear spin frequencies vary enormously and this specific number is not standard.

13. **"33% of an hour has 19 minutes and 47 seconds"** (Art. 45): 0.33×60=19.8, not 19.47. The author notes .33×60=19.8 then .8×60=47, so 19.47 is derived from 33.333...% not 33%.
