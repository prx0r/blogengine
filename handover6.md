### 2. The 36 Tattvas Are EDGES, Not Vertices

The 36 tattvas = 9 tetrahedra × 6 edges − 18 shared = 36 edges. The vertices are the 4 upāyas (invariant across all 9 tetrahedra). The 36 tattvas = the 36 edges of the 9-tetrahedron system = the 36 positive roots of SU(6).

50 = 36 tattvas (consonants, the "body") + 14 vowels (the "meta-tattvas" = 4 vertices × 3 + 2 source vowels). The 50-cell (a uniform 4D polytope) has exactly 50 vertices — the Mātṛkā ARE the vertices of this polytope.

The 30 Aethyrs = the 30 1-dimensional features of the 5-cell (4D tetrahedron). 30 + 20 = 50, bridging Enochian to the Mātṛkā.

### 5. The 64 IVM = 2⁶ = Phase Space of K₄

Ñāṇavīra's 6 edges, each tense or relaxed → 2⁶ = 64 configurations = the Isotropic Vector Matrix = 8 mirrored Trees of Life.

### 6. The 72 Shemhamphorasch = 2 × 36
