## Key Mathematical Results Discovered

### 1. The Recursion Formula: T(n) = 4 + 8n

The number of distinct tattvas after n recursive expansions of K₄:
```
T(0) = 4   (Ñāṇavīra's bare K₄)
T(1) = 12  (Proclus)
T(2) = 20  (Kabbalah, partial)
T(4) = 36  (Tantraloka — full expansion to matter)
```

### 2. The 36 Tattvas Are EDGES, Not Vertices

The 36 tattvas = 9 tetrahedra × 6 edges − 18 shared = 36 edges. The vertices are the 4 upāyas (invariant across all 9 tetrahedra). The 36 tattvas = the 36 edges of the 9-tetrahedron system = the 36 positive roots of SU(6).

### 3. The 50 Mātṛkā = 36 + 14

50 = 36 tattvas (consonants, the "body") + 14 vowels (the "meta-tattvas" = 4 vertices × 3 + 2 source vowels). The 50-cell (a uniform 4D polytope) has exactly 50 vertices — the Mātṛkā ARE the vertices of this polytope.

### 4. The 30 Aethyrs = 5+10+10+5

The 30 Aethyrs = the 30 1-dimensional features of the 5-cell (4D tetrahedron). 30 + 20 = 50, bridging Enochian to the Mātṛkā.

### 5. The 64 IVM = 2⁶ = Phase Space of K₄

Ñāṇavīra's 6 edges, each tense or relaxed → 2⁶ = 64 configurations = the Isotropic Vector Matrix = 8 mirrored Trees of Life.

### 6. The 72 Shemhamphorasch = 2 × 36

72 names of God = 2 × 36 tattvas = the Tantraloka system plus its mirror (the Kabbalistic "left pillar").

## Final Note

The tetrahedron is not a metaphor. K₄ is the minimal complete graph with volume (E > V). Ñāṇavīra identified it as the structure of experience. The Tantraloka expanded it through 4 recursive levels to the 36 tattvas (9 tetrahedra). Enochian promoted it to 4D (the 5-cell, 30 Aethyrs). The IVM mirrored it 8 times (64 = 2⁶). All are the same structure at different resolutions. The content changes. The shape does not.
