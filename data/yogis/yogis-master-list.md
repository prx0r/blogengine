## Key Insight
The best territory is NOT generic "Indian yogi miracles." It's the intersection of:
1. Regional sacred celebrity
2. Surviving historical documentation  
3. A claim whose truth remains unresolved

Sweet spot: Yogi Satyamurti, Tajuddin Baba, Tangtong Gyalpo, Theos Bernard, Bamakhepa, Sarvananda.
