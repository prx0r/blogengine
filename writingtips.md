# Writing Tips — Video Scripts

From CLAUDE4 + v6algorithm + goodprose.md + slopreview.md + becoming_an_angel_v6 reference + repeated failures on Spanda script.

## The Core

**Source passages ARE the content. I am the frame, not the picture.** The reader came for Kallata/Abhinavagupta, not for my explanation of them. v6 Angel: 8-line source blocks, 2-line AI blocks. Source carries the weight.

## 8 Mistakes I Keep Making

### 1. AI blocks too long
v6 Angel AI blocks average 30 words. Mine average 60+. Shorter AI blocks force the source to carry weight.

### 2. Source quotes chopped too short
v6: one source block = 400-900 chars, complete thought, let it breathe. Me: 1-2 lines, then back to my voice. The source never gets to speak.

### 3. Wrong ratio
v6: ~40% AI, ~60% source. AI blocks shorter. Me: ~80% AI, ~20% source. I was the main character.

### 4. NARR everywhere
"Kallata opens the Spanda Karika with..." — narrating the text instead of stating the idea. "The second chapter takes up mantra" — structural narration. v6 never does this. "Alchemy is the controlling metaphor." Full stop.

### 5. NEG everywhere
"Not X, but Y" in all 9 disguised forms. Fragment: "Not your heartbeat." Clause: "Through alignment, not force." Staging: "The wheel is not out there. The wheel is you." Each one must be replaced with a flat assertion.

### 6. Paraphrase-itis
AI blocks restating what the source just said. Every AI block must ADD what the source doesn't say. If removing it loses nothing, cut it.

### 7. Abstract over concrete
v6: "furnace, alembic," "brushwood fire," "rot before it can fruit," "whetted in intention," "bones shake." Me: "substrate of experience," "fundamental nature," "the pulse of consciousness." Every abstract noun should be replaced with a physical one.

### 8. No dominant metaphor
v6: alchemy (furnace, alembic, gold, transmutation, prima materia) runs through every block. Me: "pulse" competes with "substrate," "nature," "Reality," "consciousness" — five abstract nouns for the same thing. Pick one concrete metaphor and seed it through every block.

## The Ending Rule

A good ending circles back to the opening concretely. A bad one sums up abstractly. v6: "The imaginal is reality's deepest register, and the work of attending to it is the oldest form of philosophy." Me: "Because it is what you are." If it sounds like a tagline, rewrite.

## Verification

1. Read only AI blocks. If any restates the source, cut it.
2. Count NEG + NARR + 3LIST. If > 5, rewrite.
3. Every AI block must have one unexpected concrete noun.
4. Ending must NOT share key words with the last source passage.
5. Read aloud. If it sounds like a university lecture, rewrite.
