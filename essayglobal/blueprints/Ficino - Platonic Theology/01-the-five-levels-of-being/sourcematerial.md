# Essay 1 — The Five Levels of Being

**Source:** *Platonic Theology*, Volume 1, Book I, Chapter 1

**Context:** Book I, Chapter 1. Ficino presents the five levels of being: body (*massa corporis*), quality (*qualitas*), soul (*anima*), angel (*angelus*), and God (*deus*). The soul is the middle term that links all levels.
