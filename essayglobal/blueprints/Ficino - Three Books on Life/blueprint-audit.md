# Ficino Blueprint — Complete File Map & Purpose

Every file in the Ficino *Three Books on Life* companion project, indexed by role. Files are grouped by function, not location.

---

## Category 1: Master Documents

These files define the entire companion sequence and the process for producing it.

| File | Purpose | Read First? |
|------|---------|-------------|
| `3booksthesis.md` | **The Complete Thesis** — Master document containing all 15 essay plans (core question, thesis, structure, passage targets, overlap boundaries), plus the Prima Materia section documenting the extraction process, the Essay Writing section documenting the revision arc, and 10 key principles for essay writing. The definitive guide to the entire project. | **Yes — before any work** |
| `ficino blueprint.md` | **Original Drafting Blueprint v0.4** — Chapter-by-chapter extraction dossier with Latin anchors, short English quote anchors, passage analysis, and structural logic for each of the 15 essays. Contains the original length tiers, passage-pack targets, and overlap boundaries. The raw material from which the thesis was built. | Yes — source reference |
| `thesisideal.md` | **Quality Reference** — Collates the best examples of alive prose from the existing ficino v6 essays and Voss's thesis. Shows what "good" looks like at both sentence level (short-form AI blocks) and paragraph level (extended Voss passages). Includes the texture word palette, structural templates, and a 7-point pre-writing checklist. | Yes — before writing any essay |

## Category 3: Essay Folders (00-14)

Each essay has its own numbered folder containing three files:

| File | Purpose | Status |
|------|---------|--------|
| `essay.md` | The finished essay. Written in the V7 long-form protocol: 60% Ficino's words, 25% contextual framing, 15% analysis. TTS-readable, source-heavy, no summary endings. | Complete (100-303 lines each) |
| `sourcematerial.md` | Extracted source passages from De vita, with Latin originals, analysis, extraction status table, and post-essay review. | Complete (with post-essay reviews) |
| `draft.md` | Rough structural notes written before the essay. Contains section plans, V7 notes, and the initial argument arc. | Complete (rough notes) |
