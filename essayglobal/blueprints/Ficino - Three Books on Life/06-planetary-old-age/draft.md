# Draft — Essay 6: Planetary Old Age and the Seven-Year Dangers

## Rough Structure

### Opening
Book II begins to lean toward Book III. Old age is treated not only through diet but through planets, pleasure, scent, song.

### Section 1 — Planetary Support
II.13. Planets support bodily members. The body ages within a cosmic ecology.

### Section 2 — Venus and Mercury
Green fields, delight, odours, song. Delight as therapy. Mercury's subtle medicine.

### Section 3 — The Bridge
Odours and honey (II.18). The Medicine of the Magi (II.19). Book II already contains magian medicine.

### Section 4 — The Septenary Years
II.20, the core passage. Each seventh year brings change. Time is patterned by planetary sequence.

### Section 5 — Not Fatalism
Stars threaten and promise. Medicine intervenes. The body passes through thresholds, but care matters.

### Closing
The bridge is built. Book III can now begin.
