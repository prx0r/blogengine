# Draft — Essay 4: Why Long Life Matters to Knowledge

## Rough Structure

### Opening
Why does a philosopher care about long life? Book II is not filler. It was written separately and inserted between Books I and III. Ficino realised that repair alone is not enough.

### Section 1 — Art is Long
II.1. Judgment needs experience. Experience needs time. Short life damages knowledge. The epistemological argument.

### Section 2 — The Finite Flame
II.2–3. Vital heat and radical moisture. Life as a flame consuming its own fuel.

### Section 3 — Digestion as Renewal
II.4. The body always spending, always needing replenishment.

### Section 4 — The Knower Preserved
Longevity as infrastructure for wisdom. The task is not hoarding years but preserving the instrument.

### Closing
Book II mediates between repair and celestial opening. The instrument must last long enough for the journey.
