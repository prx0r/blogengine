# Draft — Essay 9: The Celestial Catalogue

## Rough Structure

### Opening
The world as celestial pharmacy. Ficino does not only look upward. He looks around at earthly things that carry star-powers.

### Section 1 — Matter Saturated with Spirit
III.11. World-spirit as quintessence. Things filled with purer spirit: wine, sugar, gold, precious stones, sweet smells, shining things.

### Section 2 — Cosmic Digestion
Ordinary food becomes life through spirit. Bodies drink from world-life. Book III expands Book II's digestion into cosmic ingestion.

### Section 3 — Solarisation
The solar catalogue: heliotrope, gold, honey, ram, hawk, lion. Also solar thought, solar love, solar habits. A total ecology.

### Section 4 — Jovialisation
Silver, wine, coral, sugar. Thoughts, feelings, company. Celestial influence is social.

### Section 5 — Medicines Stronger Than Images
III.15. Links back to Books I and II.

### Closing
Book I gave remedies. Book II gave longevity substances. Book III reveals them as part of a cosmic ecology.
