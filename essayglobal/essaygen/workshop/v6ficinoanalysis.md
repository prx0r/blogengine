### Key Differences from Becoming an Angel v6

1. **Block density**: Becoming has 24 body blocks (15 AI + 9 source). My best is essay 6 with 9 body blocks (5 AI + 3 source + 1 art). The ficino essays need expansion — more source passages and more interpretive AI blocks between them.

2. **Art inline**: Becoming has 7 inline art blocks. My essays have 1-2 each. Art is a tool for breaking rhythm and adding visual- conceptual depth.

3. **Texture density**: Becoming averages 1.2 texture words per AI block. My essays average 0.8. Every block needs at least one unexpected word.

4. **Pattern residue**: 7 remaining pattern violations across 6 essays (2 NEG + 5 3LIST). These need the v5→v6 treatment: identify each consciously, fix one sentence at a time, verify.

5. **Ending quality**: Becoming's closing blocks circle back to the opening thesis and resolve it. My essays either stop or end with tagline-style fragments (essay 6) or functional closes (essay 5). The ending requires the most passes.

### Repair Priority
1. Fix all 3LIST violations (5 instances) — restructure as single deepened item or merge into sentence
2. Fix NEG violations (2 instances in essay 3)
3. Add 1-2 AI blocks per essay to increase density
4. Add 1-2 inline art blocks per essay for rhythm
5. Rewrite endings for essays 4 and 5 to circle back
6. Strengthen texture in essays 3 and 4
