# Essaygen — External Perspective

## `perspective.md`

A fresh-eye critique of the v6 algorithm and all 33 v6 final essays, written by an external reviewer with no investment in the project's assumptions.

**Location:** `/root/projects/blog/perspective.md`

**Key findings:**
- Pattern elimination works but optimises for cleanliness over aliveness
- The algorithm creates a ceiling — all essays are competent, none are transcendent
- 16 of 33 essays have 4-5 AI blocks (too short to develop an argument)
- Texture word counting ≠ texture placement
- The Smuggle Test is the most original idea in the system
- The reference (becoming_an_angel v6) took 8 versions over months — the algorithm cannot shortcut this

**Read before:** Starting any new essay work. The critique identifies blind spots the algorithm doesn't catch.

**Key recommendation:** Eliminate all 4-5 block essays. Minimum 8 AI blocks per essay. A 5-block essay is a note, not an essay.
