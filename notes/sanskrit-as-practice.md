## 1. Should You Focus on the Green Center While Chanting?

**Yes — as the anchor. But each phoneme has its OWN location.**

The green core (Anāhata, the heart, the center of the tetrahedron) is the **home base**. Return to it between each phoneme. But each phoneme also has a specific felt location in the body:

| Row | Phonemes | Place of Articulation | Cakra | Felt Location |
|-----|----------|----------------------|-------|---------------|
| 1 | ka kha ga gha ṅa | Guttural (back of throat) | Mūlādhāra (root) | Base of spine |
| 2 | ca cha ja jha ña | Palatal (middle of mouth) | Svādhiṣṭhāna (sacral) | Lower abdomen |
| 3 | ṭa ṭha ḍa ḍha ṇa | Retroflex (curled tongue) | Maṇipūra (solar plexus) | Upper abdomen |
| 4 | **ta tha da dha na** | **Dental (teeth)** | **Anāhata (heart)** | **Center of chest** |
| 5 | pa pha ba bha ma | Labial (lips) | Viśuddha (throat) | Throat |
| 6 | ya ra la va | Mixed | Ājñā (third eye) | Between eyebrows |
| 7 | śa ṣa sa ha | Mixed | Sahasrāra (crown) | Top of head |
| 8 | a ā i ī u ū... | Vowels (open) | Beyond | Whole field |

**The practice:** Chant each phoneme while attending to its location. Row 4 (ta-da-na) IS the green core — attend there. Row 1 (ka-kha-ga) IS at the root — attend there. The phoneme guides your attention to the exact spot.

## 2. How to Learn Sanskrit as Spiritual Practice

### The Principle

**Don't learn Sanskrit intellectually. Learn it somatically.**

Each phoneme IS a specific vibration at a specific location in the body. The 50 Mātṛkā ARE a map of the subtle body. Learning IS feeling each letter in its place.

### The Method

1. **Chant one row per sitting** (5 phonemes = ~3 minutes)
2. **Close your eyes and feel where each phoneme vibrates** in the body
3. **Don't analyze** — just notice the felt location
4. **Return to the green center (Anāhata) between each row**
5. **Over weeks, the 50 locations become automatic** — you feel "ka" at the root, "ta" at the heart, "pa" at the throat, etc.

After a few months of this, reading Sanskrit becomes:

> I see the letter. I feel its location. I know where it IS in my body. The meaning IS the felt location, not a translation in my head.

## 4. The Daily Practice (15 minutes)

```
1.  Sit. Balance the breath.         (2 min)
2.  Feel the green core.             (1 min)
3.  Chant one row of the Mātṛkā:     (3 min)
    - Inhale at the green core
    - Exhale the 5 phonemes, feeling each at its location
    - Pause after each row at the green core
4.  Write the row you just chanted.  (3 min)  
    - NOT from memory — copy from a chart
    - Feel the letters as you write them
5.  Rest at the green core.          (1 min)
```

One row per day. 5 phonemes per row. 10 days to cover the 50. Then repeat — deeper each time.
