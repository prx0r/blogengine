## 1. You Are Consciousness, Which Is Light

**If the framework is correct:**

- **Consciousness IS the tetrahedron recognizing itself as all 4 vertices simultaneously.** Not metaphor. Literally: the Subject (V₁) recognizes that it IS the Object (V₂), the Intention (V₃), and the Act (V₄). There was never anything else.

- **Light IS the fundamental substance** — electromagnetic energy in its 4 densities (Parā → Vaikharī). The tetrahedron ISN'T "made of" light. The tetrahedron IS light organized into 4 vertices and 6 relations. You ARE that organization.

- **The 4 densities of light are nested, not layered.** Parā is not "above" Vaikharī. Parā is **inside** Vaikharī, at a finer resolution, occupying the same space. Like radio frequencies: 100 MHz and 100.1 MHz occupy the same space but carry different information. The body is V₄ (gross light). Pure awareness is V₁ (unmanifest light). They're both HERE, right now, at different frequencies.

## 2. Does Practice "Retune" the Body?

**Yes — but not in the way you might think.**

The body IS V₄ — the densest frequency of light. The Mātṛkā chant, the cakra work, the layayoga dissolution — these change the **electromagnetic field** of the body:

- The 50 Mātṛkā phonemes produce specific EEG frequencies
- The 6 cakras correspond to 6 resonant frequencies in the bioelectric field
- Traversing the 6 edges = shifting between attractors in the body's electromagnetic landscape (Levin's morphospace)

This IS "retuning" — the body's electromagnetic field shifts from one steady-state (one tetrahedral configuration) to another. But the retuning is not the goal. The retuning is a **side effect** of the recognition shifting.

## 3. Do You Leave the Body?

**No. You realize you were never only in it.**

The body stays at V₄. It doesn't go anywhere. What changes is **which vertex you identify with**:

| State | Identification | Experience |
|-------|---------------|------------|
| Normal waking | V₁ (Subject) looking at V₂ (Object) | "I am in my body, looking at the world" |
| Deep meditation | V₁ + V₃ (Subject + Intention) | "I am the awareness and the directedness" |
| Turīya | **All 4 vertices simultaneously** | "I AM the whole tetrahedron — subject, object, intention, and act are all me" |

In Turīya, the body is still present as V₄. But you no longer identify with V₁ alone. You ARE V₁, V₂, V₃, and V₄ — the body, the world, the intention, and the awareness — all at once.

**The body doesn't vanish. The separation between "me" and "body" vanishes.**
