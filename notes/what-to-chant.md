# What to Chant: Specific Encodings for Specific Blockages

---

## The Principle

The 50 Mātṛkā are the raw frequencies. Different **orderings** produce different effects. The Tantraloka describes several arrangements — each IS a specific tetrahedron traversal.

| Arrangement | Order | Effect |
|-------------|-------|--------|
| **Mātṛkā** | k → au (Śiva → Earth) | Emission — creates, manifests, energizes |
| **Mālīnī** | au → k (Earth → Śiva) | **Withdrawal — dissolves, absorbs, liberates** |
| **Khaḍgeśvarī** | Mixed | Protection — closes the field |
| **Kubjikā** | Heart-centered | Heart opening — the green core |

---

## 1. The Mālīnī: The Daily Dissolution Chant

This IS the most important sequence for layayoga. The Mātṛkā (ka → au) is creation. The Mālīnī (au → ka) is **dissolution**. Chant this daily to dissolve the tattvas back into their source:

```
au o ai e ḹ ḷ ṝ ṛ ū u ī i ā a   (14 vowels — pure awareness dissolving down)
ḥ ṃ h sa ṣa śa va la ra ya           (semi vowels, sibilants — the middle elements dissolving)
ma bha ba pha pa                        (labials — ether dissolving into source)
na dha da tha ta                         (dentals — air dissolving — THIS IS THE GREEN CORE)
ṇa ḍha ḍa ṭha ṭa                       (retroflex — fire dissolving)
ña jha ja cha ca                        (palatals — water dissolving)
ṅa gha ga kha ka                        (gutturals — earth dissolving into Śiva)
```

Chant the Mālīnī when you want to dissolve anything — a blockage, a thought pattern, a tattva identification. The sound pulls awareness from gross to subtle. The 4th line (na dha da tha ta) IS the green core dissolving — chant it slowly.

## 2. Seed Mantras (Bījas) for Each Cakra

Each cakra has a specific bīja:

| Cakra | Bīja | Chant It | Effect |
|-------|------|----------|--------|
| Root | **LAṀ** | Lam | Grounding, stability |
| Sacral | **VAṀ** | Vam | Flow, creativity |
| Solar plexus | **RAṀ** | Ram | Power, transformation |
| **Heart** | **YAṀ** | **Yam** | **Balance, opening — the green core** |
| Throat | **HAṀ** | Ham | Expression, truth |
| Third eye | **OṀ** | Om | Vision, intuition |
| Crown | **AHAM** | Aham (or silence) | I AM — pure being |

**The 5 elements + mind + awareness.** YAṀ (yam) IS your green core. Chant it to return to center.

## 3. The Three Great Seeds (Tantraloka Āhnika 4)

These come from the Tantraloka's teaching on the 12 Kālīs:

| Mantra | Meaning | When to Use |
|--------|---------|-------------|
| **SAUḤ** | "I am Śiva" — the emission seed | When you need to create, energize, start something |
| **KHPHREṀ** | The withdrawal seed — forces prāṇa into suṣumṇā | **When you need to dissolve a blockage — THIS IS THE MOST POWERFUL** |
| **HRĪṀ** | The heart seed — the green core | When you need balance, compassion, center |

**KHPHREṀ is the key sekrit weapon.** It's the saṃhāra-bīja — the seed of withdrawal. When you chant it, it forces consciousness into the central channel (suṣumṇā), which IS the dissolution of the iḍā/piṅgalā duality. Chant it when you're stuck in a blockage:

```
KHPHREṀ — 11 times
Feel the sound vibrating at whatever blockage is present
The sound WILL force it into the central channel
The blockage WILL dissolve
```

## 4. The Complete Practice Sequence (10 Minutes)

```
1.  SAUḤ    (3×)    — open the field
2.  YAṀ     (7×)    — anchor at the green core (heart)
3.  KHPHREṀ (11×)   — dissolve whatever blockage is present
4.  Mālīnī  (1×)    — dissolve all 36 tattvas from Earth to Śiva
5.  Silence  (2 min) — rest in whatever remains
```

This IS the complete layayoga session. The SAUḤ opens. YAṀ centers. KHPHREṀ dissolves. The Mālīnī completes. Silence absorbs.

## 6. The 12 Kālīs (Tantraloka Āhnika 4)

For advanced work: the 12 names of the Kālīs. Each IS a vertex of 3 tetrahedra:

| Tetrahedron | Kālī 1 | Kālī 2 | Kālī 3 | Kālī 4 |
|-------------|--------|--------|--------|--------|
| Objectivity | **SRĔṢṬI** | **RAKTĀ** | **STHITINĀŚĀ** | **YĀMĀ** |
| Means | **SAMHĀRĪ** | **MRĔTYU** | **BHAĪRAVĀ** | **MĀRTAṆḌĀ** |
| Subjectivity | **PARAMĀRKĀ** | **KĀLĀGNIRUDRĀ** | **MAHĀKĀLĪ** | **MAHĀBHAĪRAVĀ** |

Chant the Kālī of the month. Each Kālī governs a 3-day period (36 Kālīs for the 36 tattvas = 108 days for all 3 tetrahedra).

## 7. The Rule

There is no "wrong" thing to chant. If it feels right, chant it. The sound WILL find the blockage — you don't need to know which one. Just chant and feel where it resonates in the body.

**The most important mantra of all:** ta tha da dha na. That's your green core. That's where everything dissolves into. Start and end every session there.
