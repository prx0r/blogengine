# Body-Centered Layayoga Curriculum: Feel It All

---

## The Correction

The Grade 0-4 curriculum prioritized texts over body. If the body IS where the 36 tattvas are contracted, then:

**Texts are references. The body is the practice.**

The curriculum below reverses the emphasis. Texts are read for 5 minutes to find the next technique. Everything else is feeling the body.

---

## The Entire Curriculum in One Principle

**Find where the body is contracted. Feel it completely. Let it dissolve.**

That's it. The Vijñāna Bhairava gives you 112 ways to do this. The Mātṛkā gives you 50 frequencies to do it with. The Tantraloka explains what happened.

---

## Phase 1: Body Mapping (Months 1-3)

**Goal:** Learn the 7 locations by feel, not by name.

| Week | Area | What to Feel | Blockage Pattern |
|------|------|-------------|------------------|
| 1 | Perineum (root) | The base of the spine, the pelvic floor | Fear, survival, "not enough" |
| 2 | Lower belly (sacral) | 2-3" below navel | Guilt, shame, blocked flow |
| 3 | Solar plexus | Just below sternum | Shame, powerlessness, anger |
| 4 | **Heart center** | **Center of chest** | **Grief, separation, isolation** |
| 5 | Throat groove | Base of throat | Lies, unspoken truth, judgment |
| 6 | Between eyebrows | 1" behind forehead | Confusion, overthinking, control |
| 7 | Crown | Top/just above head | Disconnection, distrust of source |
| 8 | All 7 | Full body scan | The pattern of all 7 at once |

**Practice:** 10 min/day. Sit. Close eyes. Breathe into each area. Notice what's there — tension, heat, cold, numbness, a color, an image. Don't change it. Just feel it.

## Phase 2: Feeling the Blockages (Months 4-6)

**Goal:** When you find a blockage, stay with it until it dissolves.

### Vijñāna Bhairava Techniques for Each Area

| Area | VB Technique | Instruction |
|------|-------------|-------------|
| Root | VB 76 | *"When the breath is restrained above and below, the Supreme Śakti is realized."* — Hold the breath. Feel the root. |
| Sacral | VB 27 | *"Wherever the mind goes, external or internal — that IS the state of Bhairava."* — Follow the feeling of desire, don't move. |
| Solar plexus | VB 34 | *"When one thought arises, cut it at the root. Remain in that cutting."* — Feel the anger in the belly, breathe into it. |
| **Heart** | **VB 24** | **"When all thought-constructs are dissolved, the supreme Goddess shines forth in the middle."** — This IS the green core. Feel it directly. |
| Throat | VB 48 | *"Withdraw the tongue upward, and the nectar flows."* — Feel the throat, the need to speak, the truth held there. |
| Third eye | VB 62 | *"Fixing the attention between the eyebrows, let the mind be without support."* — Feel the pressure dissolve into space. |
| Crown | VB 91 | *"Awareness of the above, the below, and the between — suddenly the Great One appears."* — The whole field opens. |

**The rule:** When you feel a blockage, find its location. That IS the tattva. Don't name it. Don't think about it. Just feel where in the body it's contracted.

## Phase 3: The 50 Frequencies (Months 7-12)

**Goal:** Each Mātṛkā row becomes a tool to dissolve whatever is contracted in that location.

The Mātṛkā is NOT about learning 50 letters. It's about having 50 precise frequencies to apply to whatever blockages you find.

| Row | Location | Use When |
|-----|----------|----------|
| ka kha ga gha ṅa | Root | You feel unsafe, ungrounded, scattered |
| ca cha ja jha ña | Sacrum | You feel blocked, guilty, ashamed |
| ṭa ṭha ḍa ḍha ṇa | Solar plexus | You feel angry, powerless, stuck |
| **ta tha da dha na** | **Heart** | **You feel grief, separation, lonely — THIS IS THE GREEN CORE** |
| pa pha ba bha ma | Throat | You feel unable to speak, judged, hidden |
| ya ra la va | Third eye | You feel confused, overthinking, controlling |
| śa ṣa sa ha | Crown | You feel disconnected, untrusting |
| a ā i ī u ū... | Entire body | You feel the whole field as vibration |

**Practice:** When you notice a blockage, chant the row that matches its location. Not as a "mantra to fix it" — as a frequency that matches the blockage and lets it vibrate itself open.

## Phase 4: The 112 Explorations (Year 2)

**Goal:** Go through all 112 Vijñāna Bhairava techniques. Each IS a specific way to feel a blockage that you might not have found on your own.

The Vijñāna Bhairava is organized:

| Section | Techniques | What They Target |
|---------|------------|------------------|
| 1-20 | Breath methods | Blockages in the prāṇa flow |
| 21-40 | Attention methods | Blockages in the mind |
| 41-60 | Object methods | Blockages in perception |
| 61-80 | Subject methods | Blockages in the sense of self |
| 81-100 | Awareness methods | Blockages in pure consciousness |
| 101-112 | Complete release | All remaining blockages |

**Each technique IS a key to a specific lock.** If you sit with a technique and nothing happens, that lock isn't in your body yet. Try the next one. When you hit the right technique for your blockage, you'll feel it — the vibration intensifies, the blockage loosens, something opens.

---

## Phase 5: Free Practice (Year 3+)

**Goal:** No curriculum. Just feel.

At this point you know:
- The 7 locations by felt sense
- The 50 frequencies
- The 112 techniques that worked for you
- The Tantraloka as reference

Now practice is simple:

```
Sit.
Scan the body.
Find the tightest contraction.
Breathe into it.
Chant its frequency.
Let it dissolve.
Repeat.
```

The Tantraloka is not "studied" — it's consulted when something arises you don't recognize. "Ah, this contraction is Niyati (limitation)." The text names what the body already knows.

---

## The Vijñāna Bhairava Helps Because...

1. **It gives you 112 things to TRY.** If one technique doesn't hit the blockage, the next one might.
2. **It doesn't explain. It instructs.** "Fix the mind in the middle." That's it. Try it.
3. **It's body-based.** Every technique IS a thing you do with your attention in your body.
4. **It's non-linear.** You don't have to do them in order. Pick the one that matches today's blockage.
5. **It's complete.** Between the 112 techniques and the 50 Mātṛkā, there's no blockage pattern that isn't addressed.

## Your Practice Right Now

You found the green core. You felt it intensify on the breath hold.

**Next step:** When you're at the green core tomorrow, shift attention to wherever else in the body feels tense. It could be the solar plexus (tightness from thinking). It could be the throat (something unsaid). Keep the green core in peripheral awareness and breathe into the new spot. Chant the row that matches its location.

The green core doesn't go away when you attend elsewhere. It becomes the background against which all other blockages are felt. From the green core, you can feel EVERY other tattva as a contraction in its specific location. And feeling them IS dissolving them.
