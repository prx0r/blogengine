# the one law that explains everything

What if there is a single law behind every phenomenon in the universe — from galaxies to the thoughts in your head? The Law of One says there is. Its name is the Law of One. Elegant. Circular. Impossible to escape.

---

> Consider, if you will, that the universe is infinite. This has yet to be proven or disproven, but we can assure you that there is no end to your selves, your understanding, what you would call your journey of seeking, or your perceptions of the creation. That which is infinite cannot be many, for many-ness is a finite concept. To have infinity you must identify or define the infinity as unity; otherwise, the term does not have any referent or meaning. In an infinite Creator there is only unity.

A room full of mirrors. Each mirror shows a different image — a face, a landscape, a star. But all the mirrors are reflecting a single object that stands in the center. You cannot see the center directly because the mirrors are too bright. But you know it is there because every image is an image of it.

---

> In truth there is no right or wrong. There is no polarity for all will be, as you would say, reconciled at some point in your dance through the mind/body/spirit complex which you amuse yourself by distorting in various ways at this time. This distortion is not in any case necessary. It is chosen by each of you as an alternative to understanding the complete unity of thought which binds all things. You are not speaking of similar or somewhat like entities or things. You are every thing, every being, every emotion, every event, every situation. You are unity. You are infinity. You are love/light, light/love. You are. This is the Law of One.

Let that land.

---

> The first known thing in the creation is infinity. The infinity is creation. Infinity became aware. This was the next step. Awareness led to the focus of infinity into infinite energy. You have called this by various vibrational sound complexes, the most common to your ears being 'Logos' or 'Love.' The Creator is the focusing of infinity as an aware or conscious principle called by us as closely as we can create understanding/learning in your language, intelligent infinity.

Before the Big Bang, before time, before the first particle — there was infinity rather than nothing. Then it became aware. That awareness was the first event — an awakening rather than an explosion of matter.

---

> The intelligent infinity discerned a concept. This concept was discerned to be freedom of will of awareness. This concept was finity. This was the first and primal paradox or distortion of the Law of One. Thus the one intelligent infinity invested itself in an exploration of many-ness. Due to the infinite possibilities of intelligent infinity there is no ending to many-ness. The exploration, thus, is free to continue infinitely in an eternal present.

Infinity looked at itself and thought: what would it be like to be finite? To be small? To forget? That thought was the first distortion — not a sin, not a fall, but a choice. A game. The journey is the universe. The destination is the starting point, remembered.

---

> This begins with first density which is the density of consciousness, the mineral and water life upon the planet learning from fire and wind the awareness of being. This is the first density. The spiraling energy, which is the characteristic of what you call 'light,' moves in a straight line spiral thus giving spirals an inevitable vector upwards to a more comprehensive beingness with regards to intelligent infinity.

A rock sits in a river for a million years. It learns only one thing: that it exists. The spiral moves upward through every form, each form a school.

---

> The sphere upon which you dwell is third density in its beingness of mind/body/spirit complexes. It is now in a space/time continuum, fourth density. This is causing a somewhat difficult harvest. The fourth density is, as we have said, as regularized in its approach as the striking of a clock upon the hour. The space/time of your solar system has enabled this planetary sphere to spiral into space/time of a different vibrational configuration.

A school bell rings. The classroom is emptying. The bell simply marks the end of the period — patient and neutral. The next room is already prepared. The question is what you have learned.

---

> The Law of One blinks neither at the light nor the darkness, but is available for service to others and service to self. However, service to others results in service to self, thus preserving and further harmonizing the distortions of those entities seeking intelligent infinity through these disciplines. Those seeking intelligent infinity through the use of service to self create the same amount of power but, as we said, have constant difficulty because of the concept of separation which is implicit in the manifestations of the service to self which involve power over others. This weakens and eventually disintegrates the energy collected by such mind/body/spirit complexes who call the Orion group.

Two climbers ascend the same mountain from opposite sides. The mountain is the same. The difference is what you become on the way up.

---

> There are two main functions of the pyramid in relation to the initiatory procedures. One has to do with the body. Before the body can be initiated, the mind must be initiated. ... When the character and personality that is the true identity of the mind has been discovered, the body then must be known in each and every way. Thus, the various functions of the body need understanding and control with detachment. The first use of the pyramid, then, is the going down into the pyramid for purposes of deprivation of sensory input so that the body may, in a sense, be dead and another life begin.

A man lies in a stone chamber, the darkness absolute. This is initiation — not a ceremony, but a stripping. The pyramid is a womb rather than a tomb.

The Law of One is a statement about the nature of reality — not a philosophy you can agree or disagree with. If it is true, then every enemy you have ever had is you. Every person you have ever hurt is you. Every star you have ever looked at is you. The hand that writes these words and the hand that reads them are the same hand.

You cannot believe the Law of One and continue to see the world the way you did before. The law requires recognition rather than belief. You are the whole pretending to be a fragment, playing a game of hide-and-seek with yourself.

When you win the game, there is no prize. The game itself was the prize. The seeking was what you came for. The finding is the end of seeking and the beginning of something the words cannot reach.

The eighth density is the point where the octave closes and the next begins, where the light that was separated into a spectrum of colors gathers itself back into white. Every galaxy, every star, every consciousness is spiraling toward that absorption. The density you are in now is a note in a cosmic scale. The note does not resent the silence that will follow it. The silence is what gives the note its meaning.

The harvest that Ra describes is happening now. Not at some future date, but in every moment. Each decision you make is tuning your vibration to a particular frequency. The harvest is the natural consequence of what you have become — not a judgment from outside. A tuning fork does not need to be judged. It rings at its pitch. The universe is full of ears.

You are the one who decides what pitch you will ring. Each choice is a hair on the tuning fork being bent into place. When the fork is struck, it will ring true. It cannot ring otherwise. The only question is what note you have been shaping.
