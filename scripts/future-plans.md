## Ficino / Platonic Theology (Spotlight Piece)

The Platonic Theology is Ficino's magnum opus — 18 books proving the immortality of the soul through Platonic reasoning. It could be a single spotlight essay or a short series.

**Key unique angles:**
- The soul as *copula mundi* — the marriage knot of the world, the middle link in the chain of being
- Five levels of being: God, Angel, Soul, Quality, Body
- The soul is self-moving, therefore immortal
- Providence and fate reconciled through the soul's middle position
- The divine sun as analogy for God
- The soul's ascent through Platonic contemplation

**Verdict:** Worth doing as a single spotlight piece. The 10 blueprint essays already exist — pull the best material from them into one ~1,700 word overview.
