# Hook Audit — Points of Lost Interest

For each essay, the exact moment the energy drops. Some are clean. Most have a wound.

---

**1. The Engine of Consciousness**
Hook lands. "Before the brain. Before the mind." — strong. Lost at line 16: "Abhinavagupta, writing in the Tantraloka three centuries later, gave this pulse a precise anatomy." The moment you name Abhinavagupta and Tantraloka in the same sentence, you've left the experience and entered the lecture. The name means nothing to most people. Fix: just say the text, or better, just let the quote speak.

**2. Why You Feel Small**
Hook lands. "You can only do so much. You can only know so much." — universal. Lost at line 6: "The Tantraloka maps them one by one." Same problem. The name kills the intimacy. The reader is suddenly in a philosophy class. Fix: "A 9th-century text maps them one by one."

**3. Remember Enlightenment?**
Hook lands. "Enlightenment is something you remember." — strong. Lost at line 5: "Abhinavagupta called it the Doctrine of Reflection — pratibimbavāda." The Sanskrit term + philosopher name in one sentence is a wall. Fix: introduce the concept first, let the term appear later as a name for something already felt.

**4. The Path You Didn't Choose**
Hook lands. "Your spiritual path picked you before you ever chose it." — strong. Lost at line 5: "The Tantric tradition calls this the three Upāyas." The name kills it. Fix: describe the paths before naming them.

**5. Why Pain Is Actually Beautiful**
Hook lands. "You have felt it — watching a tragedy and feeling something like joy." — strong. Lost at line 4: "Abhinavagupta named this mechanism rasa." Same problem. The name means nothing yet. Fix: describe what rasa feels like before naming it.

**6. The World Is Not an Illusion**
Hook lands. "You have heard the story. Eastern philosophy says the world is an illusion." — strong. Lost at line 5: "One school of Tantric thought says the opposite." — "one school of Tantric thought" is weak. Vague. Academic. Fix: "A different tradition says the opposite."

**7. How Light Becomes You**
Hook lands. "Close your eyes. The surface of your skin." — strong. Clean through line 8. Lost at "The Sāṁkhya map had 25. The Śaiva masters saw it left out consciousness itself." — names without context. Fix: describe the discovery, not the discoverers.

**8. Why Some People Wake Up Overnight**
Hook lands. "The stories are everywhere. Someone struggles for years. Nothing. Then — a shift." — strong. Lost at line 4: "The Tantraloka devotes an entire chapter to it." — the name kills the mystery. Fix: "One text devotes an entire chapter to it."

**9. The Companion You've Had Since Birth**
Hook lands. "There is something with you right now that has always been with you." — strong. Clean. Lost at line 15: "Vieira argues that Ficino's daimon..." — naming scholars the reader has never heard of. Fix: skip Vieira. Let the daimon speak for itself.

**10. The Death Everyone Forgets** ✅

**11. The Universe Is a Mirror**

**12. The World Between Worlds**

**13. The Secret Life of Matter**
